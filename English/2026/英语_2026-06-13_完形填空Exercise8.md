**What Subject Matters to Students Now More Than Ever?**

In terms of him, when 9/11 broke out, he, along with his students, might have only known the **6** ______ geography of Afghanistan — located in Central and Western Asia — but was ignorant of the human issues that existed there. They need to see not just the differences in cultures, but the **10** ______. Students need to know that the kid sitting in a school in Afghanistan today probably doesn’t speak the same language, **11** ______ the same religion or live in a home that looks anything like a student in the United States, but they have a lot of things in **12** ______. They both love their families, they both want to play and they both want to learn. When we focus on the similarities instead of the differences, it changes the picture.

Our students are growing up in a(n) **13** ______ world. Nearly all business is international. Our students will never work in isolation. They need to know that the other people they work with, whether in a cubicle down the hall or on a(n) **14** ______ around the world, all have ideas and value.

## 📝 错题 #1.1

### 【题目原文】

“To tell the truth, I can remember nothing more than a day of my seventh grade when I learned basic map skills — latitude and longitude; cardinal directions, **3** ______, and how to interpret the key.”

### 【你的答案】

B. land

### 【正确答案】

A. scale

### 【错误原因分析】

这里考查地图相关词汇搭配。句中列举的是 **basic map skills**，包括：

- latitude and longitude 经纬度
- cardinal directions 基本方位
- scale 比例尺
- key 图例

**land** 表示“土地/陆地”，不是地图技能，不能和 latitude、longitude、directions、key 构成同一类并列。

### 【涉及知识点】

- 地理类词汇
- 并列结构
- 语义场一致性

### 【解题思路】

看到 **map skills** 和 **how to interpret the key**，应联想到地图四要素/地图技能中的 **scale（比例尺）**，所以选 A。

## 📝 错题 #1.2

### 【题目原文】

“We learned about landforms like **4** ______, plateaus and mountains.”

### 【你的答案】

A. peaks

### 【正确答案】

D. plains

### 【错误原因分析】

这里在列举常见地貌类型：

- plains 平原
- plateaus 高原
- mountains 山脉

**peaks** 指“山峰”，与后面的 **mountains** 意义重复，不如 **plains, plateaus and mountains** 这一组搭配自然。

### 【涉及知识点】

- 地貌类词汇
- 并列词语搭配

### 【解题思路】

空格后面已经有 **plateaus and mountains**，前面应选择另一个同级别的大类地貌，因此选 **plains**。

## 📝 错题 #1.3

### 【题目原文】

“In terms of him, when 9/11 broke out, he, along with his students, might have only known the **6** ______ geography of Afghanistan — located in Central and Western Asia — but was ignorant of the human issues that existed there.”

### 【你的答案】

B. previous

### 【正确答案】

A. physical

### 【错误原因分析】

这里形成对比：

- **physical geography** 自然地理
- **human issues** 人文问题

句意是：他们可能只知道阿富汗的自然地理位置，却不了解当地存在的人文问题。

**previous geography** 不构成自然搭配，语义也不符合上下文。

### 【涉及知识点】

- physical geography 自然地理
- human geography / human issues 人文地理/人文问题
- 对比关系

### 【解题思路】

破折号后面的 “located in Central and Western Asia” 是地理位置说明，属于自然地理范畴；后文又说 “human issues”，因此前面应选 **physical**。

## 📝 错题 #1.4

### 【题目原文】

“Heffernan himself, majoring in history in university, for instance, knew that Afghanistan had **8** ______ the Soviet Union in the 1980s, partly contributing to the end of the cold war.”

### 【你的答案】

C. took charge

### 【正确答案】

A. fought off

### 【错误原因分析】

**fight off** 表示“击退、抵抗”。

句意：阿富汗在 20 世纪 80 年代抵抗/击退了苏联，这在一定程度上促成了冷战结束。

**take charge** 表示“负责、掌管”，不能直接表达“抵抗苏联”。

### 【涉及知识点】

- 动词短语辨析
- fight off 击退，抵抗
- 历史语境理解

### 【解题思路】

根据宾语 **the Soviet Union** 和语境 “in the 1980s”，应选择表示军事/政治抵抗的短语 **fought off**。

## 📝 错题 #1.6

### 【题目原文】

“Students need to know that the kid sitting in a school in Afghanistan today probably doesn’t speak the same language, **11** ______ the same religion or live in a home that looks anything like a student in the United States, but they have a lot of things in **12** ______.”

### 【你的答案】

B. promote

### 【正确答案】

C. practise

### 【错误原因分析】

固定搭配：

$$
\text{practise a religion}
$$

表示“信奉/践行某种宗教”。

**promote a religion** 表示“推广某种宗教”，不符合这里“信仰同一种宗教”的语境。

### 【涉及知识点】

- 固定搭配
- practise a religion 信奉/践行某种宗教
- 动词词义辨析

### 【解题思路】

空格后是 **the same religion**，英语中常用 **practise a religion** 表示“信奉宗教”，因此选 C。
