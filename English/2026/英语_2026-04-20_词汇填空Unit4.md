## 📝 错题 #7

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:Unit 4 Achievements
**知识点**:语境词汇填空与词义辨析, 词性转换与形态变化, 固定搭配与介词短语, 上下文逻辑推理与语法结构分析

### 【题目原文】
Unit 4 Achievements

Exercise 1 单句生词填空（A套）

单词库：drove / runs / hatred / wrapping / armed / ties / beat / approachable / endangered / targeted / strengthen

1. A road, connecting the outpost to Horunn Milegate, ______ along the valley.
2. African elephants are ______ with their population greatly reduced due to unlawful poaching and shrinking habitat.
3. A good teacher has to be ______ with the ability to keep students motivated throughout their learning process.
4. Toscanini ______ himself as hard as he did his orchestra. If the orchestra met his demands, he would weep for joy.
5. People volunteer to express personal values related to unselfishness, to expand their range of experiences, and to ______ social relationships.
6. Philosophers believe that desire, ______ and envy are "negative emotions" which distort the mind and lead to a pursuit of power and possessions.
7. Some organizations have carried out various programs ______ at cleaning up our surroundings and awakening people's awareness of environmental protection.
8. If you suffer from depression and want to ______ it, don't skip meals, take Vitamin B and Omega-3 fatty acids, eat less sugar and carbs, and limit your intake of alcohol and caffeine.
9. They think that, in contrast to professionals in the astronomical field, which sounds a bit "high-end", community workers tend to be more ______ to the ground or, in other words, down-to-earth.
10. This year, after thousands of the city's restaurant workers lost their jobs, many Mexican people turned to ______ tamales (墨西哥玉米粉蒸肉) at home with their families, selling them in the mornings alongside corn-based drinks.

### 【选项】
- A. drove
- B. runs
- C. hatred
- D. wrapping
- E. armed
- F. ties
- G. beat
- H. approachable
- I. endangered
- J. targeted
- K. strengthen

### 【你的答案】
1. runs  2. approachable  3. targeted  4. drove  5. strengthen  6. hatred  7. armed  8. beat  9. endangered  10. wrapping

### 【正确答案】
**1. runs  2. endangered  3. armed  4. drove  5. strengthen  6. hatred  7. targeted  8. beat  9. approachable  10. wrapping  （多余词：ties）**

### 【错误原因分析】
1. 固定搭配混淆：armed with（具备）vs targeted at（针对）——第3题 armed with the ability = 具备……的能力，第7题 programs targeted at = 针对……的项目
2. 词义辨析不熟：approachable（平易近人的）vs endangered（濒危的）——第2题看到"population reduced"应选endangered，第9题看到"down-to-earth"对应approachable
3. 上下文逻辑推理不足：第9题没有利用"down-to-earth"这个关键同义解释线索
4. 语法结构分析不足：未准确判断空格在句中的成分（如表语、后置定语等），导致词性选择错误。

### 【得分】
**6/10**（错第 2、3、7、9 题）

### 【错题逐题分析】

#### ❌ 第 2 题：approachable → endangered
- **关键线索**："population greatly reduced"（种群数量大幅减少）+ "unlawful poaching"（非法偷猎）→ 直接指向**濒危**
- **思路**：看到数量减少和偷猎，第一反应应该是 endangered
- **记忆点**：endangered species = 濒危物种

#### ❌ 第 3 题：targeted → armed
- **关键线索**：固定搭配 **armed with the ability** = 具备……的能力
- **思路**：armed 作形容词修饰 teacher，表示"装备有某种能力"。targeted 后面接 at，不能接 with
- **记忆点**：be armed with = 装备有、具备

#### ❌ 第 7 题：armed → targeted
- **关键线索**：**targeted at = aimed at = 针对……**
- **思路**：programs targeted at = 针对……的项目。armed 的搭配是 armed with，不是 armed at
- **记忆点**：targeted at / aimed at = 针对

#### ❌ 第 9 题：endangered → approachable
- **关键线索**：down-to-earth（接地气的）是解释说明，前面应该是同义表达；与 high-end（高大上的）形成对比
- **思路**：approachable to the ground = 平易近人、接地气。endangered 完全不符合语境
- **记忆点**：approachable = 平易近人的、易于接近的

### 【错误原因总结】
1. **固定搭配混淆**：armed with（具备）vs targeted at（针对）——第3题和第7题正好互换了
2. **词义辨析不熟**：approachable（平易近人的）vs endangered（濒危的）——第2题和第9题正好互换了
3. **上下文逻辑推理不足**：第9题没有利用"down-to-earth"这个关键同义解释线索

### 【解题思路】
本题为单句词汇填空，需结合上下文语境、语法结构及固定搭配进行综合判断。具体解析如下：
1. 考查主谓一致与动词时态。主语为单数名词 'A road'，描述客观地理事实用一般现在时，故填 runs。
2. 考查形容词作表语。根据后半句 'population greatly reduced'（种群数量大幅减少）及 'unlawful poaching'（非法偷猎）可知，非洲象处于濒危状态，故填 endangered。
3. 考查固定搭配。armed with the ability = 具备……的能力，armed 作形容词修饰 teacher，表示"装备有"。
4. 考查动词过去式与固定搭配。drive oneself hard 意为'严格要求自己'，根据语境描述过去的人物事迹，用过去式 drove。
5. 考查动词原形。to strengthen social relationships 作目的状语，strengthen 意为'加强/巩固'，符合语境。
6. 考查名词辨析。desire, hatred and envy 并列，hatred（仇恨）为典型的负面情绪，符合 'negative emotions' 的描述。
7. 考查固定搭配。program targeted at... 意为'针对……的项目'，targeted at 为固定搭配，相当于 aimed at。
8. 考查动词不定式。want to beat it 中 beat 意为'战胜/克服'，beat depression 为常见搭配，意为'战胜抑郁'。
9. 考查形容词辨析。approachable to the ground = 平易近人、接地气，与 down-to-earth 同义呼应。与 high-end（高大上的）形成对比。
10. 考查动名词。turn to doing sth. 意为'开始做某事/转向做某事'，故填 wrapping。
