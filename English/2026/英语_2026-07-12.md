### 【逐空错误分析】

**空1 → E appointed（你填 K charge）**
- 结构：`A researcher was ___ to study...`。was + 过去分词 = 被动，"被指派去研究"。**appoint sb. to do** 固定搭配。
- charge 作动词是"控告/收费"，塞不进这里；就算 in charge 也不对，因为这里缺的是"被派去"的动词。

**空3 → K charge（你填 J indications）**
- 结构：`referees were in ___`。**be in charge** = 负责、掌管，固定短语。
- indications（迹象）是名词复数，`in indications` 不成搭配。charge 被误用到空1，导致这里错填。

**空4 → G transformed（你填 D initiated）**
- 结构：`When ___ into a standard match of 90 minutes`。把 61 个错误"换算/转换"成标准 90 分钟比赛。**transform ... into** = 转换成。
- initiate into 意思是"传授、引入（某种知识/规则）"，和"换算数据"语义不符（它属于空10）。

**空5 → I remarkably（你填 F average）**
- 结构：`a(n) ___ high number`。空格修饰形容词 high，需要**副词**。remarkably（非常地、惊人地）✓
- average 是形容词/名词，不能修饰 high。词性定位题。

**空6 → J indications（你填 B figures）**
- 结构：`and ___ are that the errors were more likely...`。**indications are that...** = "种种迹象表明……"，固定表达，主语用名词复数。
- figures（数据）虽复数，但 figures are that 不成立；figures 该用在空7。

**空7 → B figures（你填 C inevitably）**
- 结构：`The research ___ show that the best distance is about 20 meters.`
- 空格在主语 The research 和复数动词 show 之间，缺**名词复数**：the research **figures** show = "研究数据表明"，figures 与 show 主谓一致。✓
- inevitably 是副词，既不能作主语、也和复数动词 show 不搭。

**空9 → F average（你填 E appointed）**
- 结构：`The ___ speed for errors was 4 meters per second`。前文刚说 "on average"，这里指"（导致错误的）平均速度"。**average speed** = 平均速度。
- appointed（被任命的）完全跑题。

**空10 → D initiated（你填 G transformed）**
- 结构：`referees should be ___ into the rule of...`。**initiate sb. into sth.** = 使某人开始了解/传授某人某规则。指"让裁判学会'远距离盯球'的规则"。
- transformed 该用在空4。

### 【涉及知识点】
1. **被动语态识别**：`be + 过去分词`（空1、4、10）——先看是不是被动，锁定"动词过去分词"。
2. **固定搭配**：be in charge / transform...into / initiate sb. into / indications are that / appoint sb. to do。
3. **词性定位法**：空格后跟形容词(high)→填副词(remarkably，空5)；空格夹在主语与复数动词间→填名词复数(figures，空7)。
4. **连锁错误**：本卷错误呈"多米诺"式——charge 该放空3却放到空1，引发一连串错位。**做选词填空先填最有把握的"死搭配"，再回填灵活空**，能大幅减少连锁错。

### 【举一反三】
1. 词性定位：`The results were ___ consistent with our prediction.`（A. surprise B. surprising C. surprisingly）→ 空后是形容词 consistent，选 **C**。
2. 固定搭配：`The new manager was ___ into the company's core secrets on day one.`（initiate / transform / appoint）→ **initiated**（initiate sb. into sth.）。

### 【逐题错误分析】

**第3题 → D recognizable（你选 C changeable）**
- 句子：`most people's personalities in older age are barely ___ compared to their younger selves.`
- **barely recognizable** = "几乎认不出来"，呼应下文"就像换了个人"。
- changeable：barely changeable = "几乎不会变"，与全文"性格变化极大"的结论正好相反。

**第6题 → D rate（你选 C comment）**
- 句子：`the group were asked to ___ themselves on the same six personality traits`。
- **rate oneself on sth.** = 就……给自己评分，呼应前文老师给学生"评定"（第4题 assess）。
- comment 要接 on，comment themselves 不成立；这里要的是"打分/评定"。

**第10题 → A increasingly（你选 D obviously）**
- 句子：`our personality appears stable over short intervals—___ throughout adulthood.`
- **increasingly** = "越来越"：短期稳定，且成年期内越来越稳定，为下句"间隔越长关联越弱"铺垫。
- obviously 只是主观强调，缺"随时间递进"逻辑。

**第11题 → C However（你选 A Therefore）**
- 句子：`___ the longer the interval..., the weaker the relationship...`
- 前句"短期稳定"，后句"间隔越长关联越弱"，是转折 → However。

**第13题 → B sign（你选 D cause）**
- 句子：`it could be the first ___ that it's not just our cells that are being replaced...`
- **the first sign that...** = "第一个迹象表明……"，固定表达，接同位语从句。
- cause（原因）语义不对，这里说的是"迹象"。

### 【涉及知识点】
1. **逻辑连接词**（第10、11题）：判断前后句关系——转折 However，递进 increasingly/moreover，因果 therefore。
2. **固定搭配 + 同位语从句**：the first sign that... / rate oneself on...。
3. **紧扣主旨**：第3题 recognizable——全文主旨是"性格变化极大"，凡与主旨相反的词直接排除。

2. 固定搭配：`Please ___ your satisfaction on a scale of 1 to 10.`（rate / comment / remark）→ **rate**。

## 📝 错题 · Test 21 Part C（阅读理解 / 记叙抒情文）

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：阅读理解（细节理解 + 猜词 + 主旨标题）

### 【完整原文】
**C**

My dad loved pennies, especially those with the elegant *stalk* (茎) of wheat curving around each side of the ONE CENT on the back. Those were the pennies he grew up with during the Depression.

As a kid, I would go for walks with Dad, spying coins along the way—a penny here, a *dime* (一角硬币) there. Whenever I picked up a penny, he'd ask, "Is it a wheat?" It always thrilled him when we found one of those special coins produced between 1909 and 1958, the year of my birth.

One gray Sunday morning in winter, not long after my father's death in 2002, I was walking down Fifth Avenue, feeling *bereft*. I found myself in front of the church where Dad once worked. I was warmly shown in and led to a seat. Hearing Dad's favorite *A Mighty Fortress Is Our God*, I burst into tears. We'd sung that at his funeral.

After the service, I shook the *pastor's* (牧师) hand and stepped onto the side walk—and there was a penny. I bent to pick it up, turned it over, and sure enough, it was a wheat. A 1944, a year my father was serving on a ship in the South Pacific.

That started it. Suddenly wheat pennies began turning up on the sidewalks of New York everywhere. I got most of the important years: his birth year, my mom's birth year, the year he graduated from college, the year he met my mom, the year they got married, the year my sister was born. But alas, no 1958 wheat penny—my year, the last year they were made.

The next Sunday, after the service, I was walking up Fifth Avenue and spotted a penny in the middle of a crossing. Oh, no, it was a busy street; cabs were speeding by—should I risk it? I just had to get it.

A wheat! But the penny was worn, and I couldn't read the date. On arriving home, I took out my glasses and took it to the light. There was my birthday!

I found 21 wheat pennies on the streets of Manhattan in the year after my father died, and I don't think that's a coincidence.

## 📝 错题 · Test 21 Part E（阅读理解 / 说明文）

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：阅读理解（猜词 + 推断 + 主旨）

### 【完整原文】
**E**

There are desert plants which survive the dry season in the form of inactive seeds. There are also desert insects which survive as inactive *larvae* (幼虫). In addition, difficult as it is to believe, there are desert fish which can survive through years of droughts in the form of inactive eggs. These are the shrimps that live in the Mojave Desert, an intensely dry region in the south-west of the United States where shade temperatures of over 50℃ are often recorded.

The eggs of the Mojave shrimps are the size and have the appearance of grains of sand. When sufficient spring rain falls to form a lake, once every two to five years, these eggs hatch. Then the water is soon filled with millions of tiny shrimps about a millimetre long which feed on tiny plant and animal organisms which also grow in the temporary desert lake. Within a week, the shrimps grow from their original 1 millimetre to a length of about 1.5 centimetres.

Throughout the time that the shrimps are rapidly maturing, the water in the lake equally rapidly evaporates. Therefore, for the shrimps it is a race against time. By the twelfth day, however, when they are about 3 centimetres long, hundreds of tiny eggs form on the underbodies of the females. Usually by this time, all that remains of the lake is a large, muddy patch of wet soil. On the thirteenth day and the next, during the final hours of their brief lives, the shrimps lay their eggs in the mud. Then, having ensured that their species will survive, the shrimps die as the last of the water evaporates.

If sufficient rain falls the next year to form another lake, the eggs hatch, and once again the shrimps pass rapidly through their cycle of growth, adulthood, egg-laying, and death. Some years there is insufficient rain to form a lake; in this case, the eggs will remain dormant for another year, or even longer if necessary. Very, very occasionally, perhaps twice in a hundred years, sufficient rain falls to form a deep lake that lasts a month or more. In this case, the species passes through two cycles of growth, egg-laying, and death. Thus, on such occasions, the species multiplies considerably, which further ensures its survival.

### 【逐题错误分析】

**第2题 → A inactive（你选 C alert）**
- 猜词：dormant = 休眠的、不活动的。虾卵在干旱期休眠、等下雨才孵化 → inactive。
- alert（警觉的、活跃的）意思正好相反。
- 词根助记：dormant 与 dormitory（宿舍）同源，dorm- 表"睡" → 休眠。

**第4题 → B the survival of desert shrimps（你选 A the life span）**
- 主旨题：全文核心是"沙漠虾如何靠休眠卵在干旱中生存" → survival 是主线。
- A "life span（寿命）"只是细节，以偏概全；C 太宽泛；D 跑偏（重点非"水的重要性"）。

### 【涉及知识点】
1. **猜词题**（第2题）：扣语境 + 词根（dorm-=睡→休眠→inactive），警惕反义干扰项。
2. **主旨题**（第4题）：抓高频核心词，排除"以偏概全/过于宽泛/偏离重点"三类干扰。
3. **推断题**（第3题，做对）：从"独特生命节律适应恶劣环境"归纳，符合科学说明文常见立意。

### 【举一反三】

2. 主旨题：科普说明文常以"某物种/现象如何适应或应对某挑战"为主旨，选项优先含高频核心词。

**得分：3/4**（只错第 4 题）

### 【逐题分析】

**第1题 ✅ → A**
- 猜义：`you'll make it` 在此语境（做志愿者、给他人生活带来改变）意为"你会做到 / 你会让它有意义"，呼应开头 made a real difference in someone's life → A。
- D "成功得到这份工作"是 make it 的另一常见义，但脱离本文"帮助他人"的语境，属于干扰。

**第2题 ✅ → D**
- 定位 Role 栏：`your primary concern is to let them know they are valued`（首要任务是让他们知道自己是被重视的）→ D to realize their own importance。

**第3题 ✅ → B**
- 定位 Skills 栏末句：`Previous care-related experience will be a great advantage for you`（有相关护理经验者优先）→ B 做过类似工作者最先被选。
- A 会开车、D 会英语都是"必备要求"而非"优先"；C 耐心倾听是基本要求。区分"required（必需）"和"advantage（加分/优先）"是关键。

**第4题 → B an advertisement for helpers（你选 C a document on appealing for volunteers）**
- 体裁/功能题。全文是一则"招募志愿者护理助理"的广告——有职位、地点、工时、职责、任职要求、申请方式，典型招募广告格式。
- B"招募帮手的广告"精准命中体裁(advertisement)+目的(找 helpers)。
- C 错在两点：① 体裁是"广告"而非"文件/document"；② appeal for（呼吁）偏情感号召，本文是实打实招人。
- 技巧：看文本长相——有职位名/任职要求/申请方式 = advertisement，不是 report/document/letter。

### 【涉及知识点】
1. **猜义题**（第1题）：短语义须扣具体语境（make it 在"帮助他人"语境=做到有意义的事）。
2. **细节定位**（第2、3题）：primary concern→首要职责；区分 required（必需）vs advantage（优先/加分）。
3. **体裁功能题**（第4题）：招募广告有职位+要求+申请方式 → advertisement，排除 document/appeal。

### 【举一反三】
1. 体裁判断：含 Position / Place / Hours / How to apply 的文本 → 招募广告(advertisement)。
2. required vs advantage：题问"谁优先被选"，找 advantage/preferred 附近的条件，而非 must/required 的硬性条件。
