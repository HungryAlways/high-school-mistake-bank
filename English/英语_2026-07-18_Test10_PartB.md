# Test 10 Part B：北斗卫星导航系统

## 题目要求

根据上下文，从每题的 A、B、C、D 四个选项中选出最合适的单词或短语。

## 完整原文

Competition is good for businesses. In the world of navigation（导航）systems, however, competition is also a necessity—it may not be wise to rely on foreign systems for positioning and tracking services. Now, **(1) ______** remarkable accuracy and reliability, China’s BeiDou system has made its presence felt.

The BeiDou project was set up in 1994. The first BeiDou satellite was not launched until 2000. Now, **(2) ______**, there are already more than 20 BeiDou satellites in orbit. They form a **(3) ______** network that provides positioning, navigation and timing services for China and several other Asian countries.

This “home-grown” system is now **(4) ______** a major upgrade. Earlier this month, two BeiDou-3 satellites, the first of China’s most powerful **(5) ______** of navigation satellites, were launched into space. The launch marks the beginning of the global **(6) ______** of the BeiDou navigation system. Over the next three years, China plans to send up 30 more BeiDou-3 satellites. The expanded navigation system will **(7) ______** create a network that is able to support military and civilian applications around the world.

Scientists involved in the project said the new system would give civilian users an accuracy of 2.5 meters to five meters, overtaking that of the **(8) ______** positioning technologies. BeiDou’s chief designer said the new satellites would be able to **(9) ______** which lane a car is using on a motorway and **(10) ______** the swing of a building in high winds. It will also be able to guide fire trucks to the nearest water hydrant（消防栓）. The Chinese military, meanwhile, will be able to use coded signals for millimeter（毫米） **(11) ______**.

China is only the third country in the world to develop a navigation system on its own, after the United States（GPS）and Russia（GLONASS）. Developing BeiDou is a necessity. The system **(12) ______** national security by ending a reliance on foreign systems. Moreover, it enhances China’s international reputation for technological **(13) ______**.

For most of us, the benefits of the new satellite system will be felt in a couple of years when more phones are **(14) ______** with BeiDou chips. Many smartphones today still use GPS and GLONASS. That’ll soon change with the development of BeiDou. One product manager **(15) ______** most smartphones to be able to receive BeiDou signals. He says: “In three years’ time, people may still say ‘I’m using GPS’, but in fact, their phone is tuned in to BeiDou.”

## 错误题号

第 **2、3、4、12题**，共4题。

## 错误分析

### 第2题：afterwards → however

原句：

> The first BeiDou satellite was not launched until 2000. Now, ______, there are already more than 20 BeiDou satellites in orbit.

前句强调第一颗卫星直到2000年才发射，后句却说明现在已经有20多颗卫星，构成“起步较晚，但发展迅速”的转折关系，因此应选 **however**。

`afterwards` 只表示“后来、之后”，不能准确表达前后对比。

**错误类型：**逻辑关系判断错误；连接副词辨析错误。

### 第3题：continental → regional

原句：

> They form a ______ network that provides positioning, navigation and timing services for China and several other Asian countries.

服务范围是中国及其他几个亚洲国家，属于一定区域，因此应选 **regional**，构成 `a regional network`。

`continental` 表示“大陆的、洲范围的”，范围通常更广，不符合这里的具体服务范围。

**错误类型：**词义辨析错误；范围判断错误。

### 第4题：processing → undergoing

原句：

> This “home-grown” system is now ______ a major upgrade.

固定搭配是：

> **undergo an upgrade**：经历升级、正在升级

主语是北斗系统，表示该系统正在经历一次重大升级。`process` 通常表示处理数据、材料或事务，不能自然表达系统自身升级。

**错误类型：**固定搭配错误；动词词义辨析错误。

### 第12题：insures → strengthens

原句：

> The system ______ national security by ending a reliance on foreign systems.

正确搭配是：

> **strengthen national security**：加强国家安全

北斗系统通过减少对外国导航系统的依赖，加强国家安全。

`insure` 主要表示“给……投保”，容易与 `ensure`（确保）混淆，但本题选项并不是 `ensures`。

**错误类型：**近形词混淆；单词词义记忆错误；固定搭配错误。

## 综合错误原因

1. 对句间转折关系判断不够准确，未识别第2题中“起步较晚，但目前发展迅速”的对比。
2. 对范围形容词辨析不够准确，混淆 `continental` 与 `regional`。
3. 固定搭配掌握不牢：`undergo an upgrade`、`strengthen national security`。
4. 混淆近形词 `insure` 与 `ensure`，未结合选项实际含义判断。

## 正确解题思路

1. 先判断空格在句中的词性和句法功能。
2. 优先识别固定搭配，如：
   - boast accuracy
   - undergo an upgrade
   - global expansion
   - millimeter accuracy
   - strengthen national security
   - technological innovation
   - be equipped with
   - expect sb. to do sth.
3. 判断上下文逻辑关系，尤其是转折、递进、因果和范围限定。
4. 将选项放回全文通读，检查语义和搭配是否自然。

## 易错提醒

- `however` 表示转折；`afterwards` 表示时间上的“后来”。
- `regional` 表示区域性的；`continental` 表示大陆或洲范围的。
- `undergo an upgrade` 是固定表达。
- `insure` 是“投保”；`ensure` 是“确保”；`strengthen` 是“加强”。
- `be equipped with` 表示“配备有”。
- `expect sb. to do sth.` 表示“预计某人或某物会做某事”。
