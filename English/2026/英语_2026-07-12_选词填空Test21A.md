## 📝 错题 · Test 21 Part A（选词填空 / 十一选十）

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：选词填空（Word Bank）

### 【词库选项】
> A. involved　B. figures　C. inevitably　D. initiated　E. appointed　F. average
> G. transformed　H. reasonable　I. remarkably　J. indications　K. charge
> （共 11 词，用 10 个，多 1 个）

### 【题目原文】
Long after the 2010 World Cup was won, disappointed fans were still criticizing the unfair refereeing decisions that denied victory to their team. A researcher was **__(1)__** to study the performance of some top referees.

The researcher organized experimental matches which **__(2)__** four youth teams. Each match lasted an hour, divided into three periods of 20 minutes during which different referees were in **__(3)__**. Observers noted down the referees' errors, of which there were 61 over the matches. When **__(4)__** into a standard match of 90 minutes, each referee made almost 23 mistakes, a(n) **__(5)__** high number.

The researcher then studied the videotapes to have a detailed analysis of the matches, and **__(6)__** are that the errors were more likely when the referees were close to the incident. When the officials got it right, they were, on average, 17 meters away from the action. The average distance in the case of errors was 12 meters. The research **__(7)__** show that the best distance is about 20 meters.

There also seemed to be a(n) **__(8)__** speed. Correct decisions came when the referees were moving at a speed of about 2 meters per second. The **__(9)__** speed for errors was 4 meters per second.

If FIFA, football's international ruling body, wants to improve the standard of refereeing at the World Cup, referees should be **__(10)__** into the rule of keeping their eyes on the action from a distance, rather than rushing to keep up with the ball, the researcher argues.

### 【逐空错误分析】

**空1 → E appointed（你填 K charge）**
- 结构：`A researcher was ___ to study...`。was + 过去分词 = 被动，"被指派去研究"。**appoint sb. to do** 固定搭配。
- charge 作动词是"控告/收费"，塞不进这里；就算 in charge 也不对，因为这里缺的是"被派去"的动词。

**空3 → K charge（你填 J indications）**
- 结构：`referees were in ___`。**be in charge** = 负责、掌管，固定短语。
- indications（迹象）是名词复数，`in indications` 不成搭配。charge 被误用到空1，导致这里错填。

**空4 → G transformed（你填 D initiated）**
- 结构：`When ___ into a standard match of 90 minutes`。把 61 个错误"换算/转换"成标准 90 分钟比赛。**transform ... into** = 转换成。
- initiate into 意思是"传授、引入（某种知识/规则）"，和"换算数据"语义不符（它属于空10）。

**空5 → I remarkably（你填 F average）**
- 结构：`a(n) ___ high number`。空格修饰形容词 high，需要**副词**。remarkably（非常地、惊人地）✓
- average 是形容词/名词，不能修饰 high。词性定位题。

**空6 → J indications（你填 B figures）**
- 结构：`and ___ are that the errors were more likely...`。**indications are that...** = "种种迹象表明……"，固定表达，主语用名词复数。
- figures（数据）虽复数，但 figures are that 不成立；figures 该用在空7。

**空7 → B figures（你填 C inevitably）**
- 结构：`The research ___ show that the best distance is about 20 meters.`
- 空格在主语 The research 和复数动词 show 之间，缺**名词复数**：the research **figures** show = "研究数据表明"，figures 与 show 主谓一致。✓
- inevitably 是副词，既不能作主语、也和复数动词 show 不搭。

**空9 → F average（你填 E appointed）**
- 结构：`The ___ speed for errors was 4 meters per second`。前文刚说 "on average"，这里指"（导致错误的）平均速度"。**average speed** = 平均速度。
- appointed（被任命的）完全跑题。

**空10 → D initiated（你填 G transformed）**
- 结构：`referees should be ___ into the rule of...`。**initiate sb. into sth.** = 使某人开始了解/传授某人某规则。指"让裁判学会'远距离盯球'的规则"。
- transformed 该用在空4。

### 【涉及知识点】
1. **被动语态识别**：`be + 过去分词`（空1、4、10）——先看是不是被动，锁定"动词过去分词"。
2. **固定搭配**：be in charge / transform...into / initiate sb. into / indications are that / appoint sb. to do。
3. **词性定位法**：空格后跟形容词(high)→填副词(remarkably，空5)；空格夹在主语与复数动词间→填名词复数(figures，空7)。
4. **连锁错误**：本卷错误呈"多米诺"式——charge 该放空3却放到空1，引发一连串错位。**做选词填空先填最有把握的"死搭配"，再回填灵活空**，能大幅减少连锁错。

### 【举一反三】
1. 词性定位：`The results were ___ consistent with our prediction.`（A. surprise B. surprising C. surprisingly）→ 空后是形容词 consistent，选 **C**。
2. 固定搭配：`The new manager was ___ into the company's core secrets on day one.`（initiate / transform / appoint）→ **initiated**（initiate sb. into sth.）。
