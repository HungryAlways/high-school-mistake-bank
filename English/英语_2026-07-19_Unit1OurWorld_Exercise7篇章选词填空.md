Over a decade ago, with the development of smartphones, film director Kevin Macdonald invited people to film whatever they wished on July 24, 2010. Many years later, cell phone camera quality has dramatically improved. So, *Life in a Day (2020)*, again __(2)__ by Macdonald, has a lot going on in its scenes about daily routines on July 25, 2020. Eventually, Macdonald and his team received a large number of videos from 324,705 __(3)__ in 65 languages from 192 different countries. Although many materials __(4)__ in by people around the world were worth watching, the director and his team tried to edit the film based on a common theme — they wanted to produce a film about love, death and heartbreak, hoping that it would look beyond geography to explore what __(5)__ us as humans.

*Life in a Day (2020)* begins with women in labor bringing new life into the world. At the crack of dawn, people awaken. It will be a day of work and play, celebrations and funerals, birth and death, which __(6)__ what the truest life is like.

Movingly, one woman plays a video of her son in a funny __(7)__ from *Life in a Day (2010)*. Then her camera goes to an urn (骨灰盒), where her son's ashes are contained. Despite the hardships, the film eventually offers a glimmer of hope that this nightmare year may have provided pause for thought — what is it that really __(10)__ in our current life as life seems too *fragile*（脆弱的）?

The two "Life in a Day" films will be better if produced as a series. And people are hoping for a follow-up in the decades beyond. Let's wait and see.

### 【错误原因分析】

- **空5 → A. connects**：explore what ___ us as humans（探究什么把我们人类联结在一起）。connect sb 联系、连接，符合共同人性主题。填 reflect 语义不对。
- **空6 → I. reflect**：..., which ___ what the truest life is like（反映最真实的生活）。reflect 反映，which 指前面生死庆典场景作主语。填 closely（副词）语法语义都不对。
- 💡 核心问题：把 5、6 两空的 reflect 位置放错——reflect 应填空6，空5 要 connects。属"词用对、位置放错"，需结合语法角色+语义双重确认。

### 【涉及知识点】
1. connect（联结）vs reflect（反映）动词辨析
2. 定语从句 which 作主语后接谓语动词
3. 选词填空解题：语法角色（词性）+ 语义搭配双重定位
4. culturally diverse 副词修饰形容词

### 【举一反三】

1. Music is a language that ____ people from different countries.（connects / reflects）
   （答案：connects）

2. His pale face ____ his poor health.（connects / reflects）
   （答案：reflects）

3. （选词填空语法定位）在句中 "which ___ the situation" 空格该填什么词性？
   （答案：动词，作 which 引导定语从句的谓语，如 reflects）
