## 📝 错题 #4

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:综合英语 · 篇章选词填空
**知识点**:词性预判、固定搭配、上下文逻辑呼应、近义词辨析

### 【题目原文】

**Exercise 7 篇章选词填空**

A. opportunities | B. alarming | C. multiply | D. funded | E. threatened | F. mirroring | G. measures | H. globally | I. exchange | J. significantly | K. figure

**The Aging Global Population**

Some people fear that the earth may "explode" with the rapid growth of world population. However, despite the **(1)** ______ rate at which the world population has been increasing in the past decade, statistics have recently shown a relatively slower growth of world population - in fact, the global population is aging.

That is the result of the continued decline in fertility rates and increase in life expectancy. This demographic (人口统计的) change has resulted in increasing proportions of people who are over 60. As a result, the first time in history when the **(2)** ______ of old citizens' population exceeds that of younger people is rapidly approaching. To deal with this tricky issue well, WHO is currently doing its duty.

WHO has developed guidelines to help countries understand the types of programmes they can put in place **(3)** ______ to improve healthcare settings, so that they are better suited to the needs of older people. WHO has **(4)** ______ networks where countries can share technical information and discuss strategies that have worked in their country to improve the lives of older people.

Moreover, studies on global aging and adult health is being undertaken by WHO around the world, **(5)** ______ the health implications of aging, particularly in less developed countries. The study helps to establish a relevant international network of health and demographic surveillance (人口统计监测) to **(6)** ______ useful data - comparisons among studies in both high and low income countries will eventually provide **(7)** ______ to understand similarities and differences in the well-being of older adults in different living environments so that better policies can be carried out.

As to the old, they are still playing their roles in society **(8)** ______. A high percentage of older people care for their grandchildren or other relatives, especially in poor regions. In Africa, for example, the family support of grandparents plays a(n) **(9)** ______ important role in supporting children, as the parents' generation has been overwhelmed with their work in big cities, and to make matters worse, some are even **(10)** ______ with violence, racism, drugs and HIV/AIDS in slums or streets.

### 【你的答案】

> 1.B · 2.A · 3.F · 4.D · 5.C · 6.K · 7.G · 8.I · 9.J · 10.E

### 【正确答案】

> 1.**B** · 2.**K** · 3.**C** · 4.**D** · 5.**F** · 6.**I** · 7.**A** · 8.**H** · 9.**J** · 10.**E**

### 【答题统计】

**6/10 正确** -- 错了 5 道(第 2、3、5、6、8 题)

### 【错题逐题分析】

#### ❌ 第 2 题:A → K
> "the **(2)** ______ of old citizens' population exceeds that of younger people"

**为什么选 K(figure):**
- **figure** = 数字、数量
- **the figure of... population** = ......人口的数量
- 句意:老年人口数量首次超过年轻人口数量的时刻正迅速逼近

❌ **你的答案问题**:opportunities(机会)填进去变成"老年人口的机会超过年轻人",语义完全不通。注意 the + 名词 + of 结构,需要抽象名词。

---

#### ❌ 第 3 题:F → C
> "programmes they can put in place **(3)** ______ to improve healthcare settings"

**为什么选 C(measures):**
- 固定搭配 **put in place measures** = 落实/实施措施
- measures = 措施,programmes 和 measures 是并列/补充关系
- 句意:各国可以落实哪些**措施**来改善医疗环境

❌ **你的答案问题**:mirroring 是分词,不能作名词用。词性就不对。先判断空格需要名词,再在选项中筛选。

---

#### ❌ 第 5 题:C → F
> "WHO around the world, **(5)** ______ the health implications of aging"

**为什么选 F(mirroring):**
- 现在分词作**伴随状语**
- mirroring = 反映、体现、与......相呼应
- 句意:WHO 在全球开展研究,**反映出**老龄化带来的健康影响

❌ **你的答案问题**:multiply(增加、繁殖)语义不通--研究不能"繁殖"健康影响。

---

#### ❌ 第 6 题:K → I
> "to **(6)** ______ useful data"

**为什么选 I(exchange):**
- **exchange data** = 交换数据,固定搭配
- to + 动词原形,空格需要**动词原形**
- 句意:建立网络以**交换**有用数据

❌ **你的答案问题**:figure 作动词意为"认为、估算、扮演",不能跟 data 搭配。

---

#### ❌ 第 7 题:G → A
> "will eventually provide **(7)** ______ to understand similarities and differences"

**为什么选 A(opportunities):**
- **provide opportunities to do sth.** = 提供做某事的机会
- 比较研究最终将**提供机会/契机**,让人们理解不同环境下的老年人福祉异同

❌ **你的答案问题**:measures(措施)搭配不自然--措施是用来"实施"的,不是"提供以理解"的。

---

#### ❌ 第 8 题:I → H
> "they are still playing their roles in society **(8)** ______"

**为什么选 H(globally):**
- 需要**副词**修饰整句
- globally = **在全球范围内**,呼应全文 global aging 的主题
- 句意:老年人**在全球范围内**仍在发挥社会作用

❌ **你的答案问题**:exchange 是动词/名词,**不能作副词**。词性错误。

---

### 【错误原因总结】

1. **词性判断不够敏锐**:第 3、6、8 题都没先判断空格需要什么词性,导致选了词性不对的选项。
2. **固定搭配积累不足**:put in place measures、exchange data、provide opportunities to do 等需要系统整理。
3. **上下文呼应意识不够**:第 2 题 figure = number(人口数量),第 5 题 mirroring = 反映,第 8 题 globally 呼应 global aging--做题要**上下扫视**找线索。

**变式练习:**

> The global population is aging **______(1)______**. As the **(2)** ______ of elderly people continues to rise, governments need to take **(3)** ______ to address the challenge. Studies **(4)** ______ (conduct) worldwide are **(5)** ______ the health implications of aging. These studies help countries **(6)** ______ (exchange / share) data and provide **(7)** ______ for better policies to be made.

<details>
<summary>点击查看答案</summary>

1. **globally**(副词,修饰整句,呼应 global aging)
2. **figure**(the figure of = ......的数量)
3. **measures**(take measures = 采取措施)
4. **conducted**(被动,被开展的研究)
5. **mirroring**(现在分词作伴随状语)
6. **exchange / share**(交换/分享数据)
7. **opportunities**(provide opportunities = 提供机会)
