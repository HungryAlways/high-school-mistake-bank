## 📝 错题 #3

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:综合英语 · 语法篇章填空
**知识点**:定语从句、让步状语从句、固定搭配、动词不定式、被动语态、强调句型

### 【题目原文】

**Exercise 6 语法篇章填空**

**How to Manage Time Well in Senior High School?**

I am involved in about a million activities inside and outside of school. I take six classes, four <u>1______</u> the others are APs, and I also work at least four days a week. I am a figure skater, which means I wake up at 4:00 am and practice for two hours four times a week. I also run a club that gives free music lessons to young students for two hours weekly. Fully occupied <u>2_______</u> I am a second-semester senior, I have done it all, and let me tell you time management is important! No matter <u>3______</u> your focus is, managing your time effectively is the key to being successful. Here are some easy tips on how to have your time well organized.

First, use a planner. In your planner, go through your calendar and write down all your activities. Even if <u>4____</u> (prefer) to use your phone calendar, you'd better copy things down into your planner's calendar, which is helpful in having everything in a tangible source. Then you can go through the week-by-week sections and write down what events you have <u>5______</u> homework assignments, exam dates, and general to-do lists. This will help make sure you know exactly what is on your schedule and when.

Second, use reminders on your phone. The Reminders app on my iPhone has been one of my main methods of organization at home. I don't normally take my planner home with me, so when I need to remember <u>6______</u> (do) something when I get home, I put it in Reminders. This app is also great for trivial things that you don't consider worthwhile to put in your planner. The alert notification only goes away when you mark the task as "completed", so you can keep the alert on your lock screen until you finish your task to remind you that it wants handling. There are various alternatives for Androids as well <u>7______</u> accomplish the same function. However, for a student who tends to be addicted to smartphones, a phone at home is likely to be a distraction. To deal with this problem, he <u>8_______</u> as well buy two phones, one for entertainment which should never be allowed in your study or bedroom, one for the role of a reminder, where distracting social media and game apps should not be allowed <u>9_____</u> (install).

Finally, I want to remind you that organization cannot be a one-time hobby. It is <u>10______</u> that you have to stay committed to. Getting started can be difficult, but once you get in the swing of organization, you will wonder why you have waited so long! I promise it will be worth your while, and in the long run it will teach you lots of skills that will be important when you leave school and get a job. For now, just take baby steps, and you'll do great.

### 【你的答案】
> 1. **the others** · 2. **for** · 3. **No matter how** · 4. **preferring** · 5. **like** · 6. **to do** · 7. **as** · 8. **though** · 9. **installing** · 10. **what**

### 【正确答案】
> 1. **of which** · 2. **though** · 3. **No matter what** · 4. **preferring** · 5. **like** · 6. **to do** · 7. **that** · 8. **may / might** · 9. **being installed** · 10. **something / what**

### 【答题统计】
**5/10 正确** -- 对了 5 道,有 5 道语法结构错误

### 【错题逐题分析】

#### ❌ 第 1 题:the others → of which
> "I take six classes, four <u>______</u> the others are APs"

**为什么选 of which:**
- **four of which** = "其中四门",which 指代 six classes
- 这是**定语从句**结构:先行词 six classes → 关系代词 which
- 完整句意:"我选了六门课,其中四门是 AP 课程"

❌ **你的答案问题**:the others 语义重复--"four the others the others are APs" 不通顺

---

#### ❌ 第 2 题:for → though
> "Fully occupied <u>______</u> I am a second-semester senior"

**为什么选 though:**
- **though** 引导让步状语从句 = "虽然"
- 这里用的是**倒装让步句**:形容词 + though + 主语 + 谓语
  - Fully occupied though I am = 虽然我完全忙碌
  - 类似:Rich as he is(虽然他很富有)、Young as she is(虽然她很年轻)

❌ **你的答案问题**:for 表原因,但句意是"虽然忙碌"而非"因为忙碌"

---

#### ❌ 第 3 题:No matter how → No matter what
> "No matter <u>______</u> your focus is"

**为什么选 what:**
- **what** 在此作 is 的**表语**(主语补足语)
- "your focus is what" → "what your focus is"(无论你的焦点是什么)
- **为什么不是 how**:how 是副词,修饰形容词/副词,不能作 is 的表语。如果是 how,后面需要跟形容词,如 "no matter how busy you are"
- 类似搭配:no matter what + 名词/形容词,no matter where/when/who

---

#### ❌ 第 7 题:as → that
> "There are various alternatives for Androids as well <u>______</u> accomplish the same function."

**为什么填 that:**
- **that** 引导**定语从句**,修饰 alternatives
- 先行词 alternatives 指物,关系代词用 that/which
- that 在从句中作**主语**(that accomplish),不可省略
- 句意:"安卓也有各种替代品,它们能实现同样的功能"

---

#### ❌ 第 8 题:though → may / might
> "he <u>______</u> as well buy two phones"

**为什么填 may / might:**
- **may as well / might as well** = "不妨,倒不如",表示建议
- 类似表达:had better, should
- 句意:"他不妨买两部手机"

❌ **你的答案问题**:though 不能和 as well 搭配使用

---

#### ❌ 第 9 题:installing → being installed
> "social media and game apps should not be allowed <u>______</u> (install)"

**为什么填 being installed:**
- allow sth **to be done** = "允许某物被......"(被动)
- social media and game apps 是**被安装**的对象,不是主动安装
- 所以用被动形式:**being installed**(allow 后可接 doing,但必须是被动)
- 句意:"不应允许安装具有干扰性的社交媒体和游戏应用"

---

### ✅ 做对的题

**第 4 题(preferring)**:Even if 后接现在分词,构成省略结构,正确!

**第 5 题(like)**:like 作介词 = "例如",后接名词短语作例子,正确!

**第 6 题(to do)**:remember to do = "记得去做"(动作未做),正确!

**第 10 题(what)**:It is what that... = "......就是......",what 引导表语从句,正确!

---

### 【错误原因总结】

1. **定语从句关系代词遗漏**:第 1 题 of which、第 7 题 that,需注意先行词为物时关系代词不可省略
2. **倒装让步句不熟**:形容词 + though + 主谓(第 2 题)
3. **固定搭配不熟**:may as well(不妨)、no matter what(无论什么)(第 3、8 题)
4. **被动语态**:allow sth to be done / being done(第 9 题)

### 💡 举一反三

**变式练习:**
> I know ___(1)___ you have to study hard. It is ___(2)___ that you need a good plan. Even if you ___(3)___ (prefer) to use an app, you should also use a planner. There are many tools ___(4)___ can help you manage time. You should ___(5)___ well try both methods. Remember ___(6)___ (write) down your tasks every day. This is ___(7)___ all successful students do. No matter ___(8)___ busy you are, organization is key.

<details>
<summary>点击查看答案</summary>

1. **what**(no matter what = 无论什么,此处作 know 的宾语从句引导词)
2. **true**(It is true that... 句型)
3. **prefer**(even if 后接完整主谓)
4. **that**(定语从句修饰 tools,指物)
5. **should**(should as well = 不妨)
6. **to write**(remember to do = 记得去做)
7. **what**(no matter what all successful students do = 无论成功的学生们做什么)
8. **how**(no matter how busy = 无论多忙)
