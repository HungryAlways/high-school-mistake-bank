## 📝 错题 #1.1

### 【题目原文】

“To tell the truth, I can remember nothing more than a day of my seventh grade when I learned basic map skills — latitude and longitude; cardinal directions, **3** ______, and how to interpret the key.”

### 【你的答案】

B. land

### 【正确答案】

A. scale

### 【错误原因分析】

这里考查地图相关词汇搭配。句中列举的是 **basic map skills**，包括：

- latitude and longitude 经纬度
- cardinal directions 基本方位
- scale 比例尺
- key 图例

**land** 表示“土地/陆地”，不是地图技能，不能和 latitude、longitude、directions、key 构成同一类并列。

### 【涉及知识点】

- 地理类词汇
- 并列结构
- 语义场一致性

### 【解题思路】

看到 **map skills** 和 **how to interpret the key**，应联想到地图四要素/地图技能中的 **scale（比例尺）**，所以选 A。

## 📝 错题 #1.3

### 【题目原文】

“In terms of him, when 9/11 broke out, he, along with his students, might have only known the **6** ______ geography of Afghanistan — located in Central and Western Asia — but was ignorant of the human issues that existed there.”

### 【你的答案】

**previous geography** 不构成自然搭配，语义也不符合上下文。

### 【涉及知识点】

- physical geography 自然地理
- human geography / human issues 人文地理/人文问题
- 对比关系

### 【解题思路】

破折号后面的 “located in Central and Western Asia” 是地理位置说明，属于自然地理范畴；后文又说 “human issues”，因此前面应选 **physical**。

## 📝 错题 #3

**学科**：英语 | **题型**：语法填空 | **主题**：有人吃掉价值 12 万美元的 Art Basel 香蕉艺术品

### 【完整原文】

**Week 14 · Grammar**

**Some Guy Ate that $120,000 Art Basel Banana**

Installations by Italian artist Maurizio Cattelan are famously inspiring, but his signature work — a banana **(1) ______** *(attach)* to a wall — fell prey to a basic urge: the hunger it aroused in a South Korean college student.

The art in question, *Comedian*, is a frequently replaced wall-taped banana that is meant to evoke everything from Charlie Chaplin’s slapstick comedy to the fruit’s status as a symbol of global trade. However, it spoke to Noh Hyun-soo in **(2) ______** *(simple)* terms, reminding him that he **(3) ______** *(skip)* breakfast that morning. So Noh seized the yellow fruit and ate it, **(4) ______** *(ignore)* the alarmed cry of a museum staffer. **(5) ______** took Noh around 1 minute to grab the banana and eat it. When he was done, he reattached the peel to its spot on the wall.

Noh told the museum that he ate the art because he was hungry, according to Korean public broadcaster KBS, **(6) ______** aired a video of his actions recorded by his friend. But Noh, who studies aesthetics and religion at Seoul National University, also expressed his view **(7) ______** the artist’s intention in an interview with KBS, asking **(8) ______** the fruit — which is replaced every few days to keep the installation looking fresh — is meant to be eaten. Noh also suggested his own actions might qualify as art, rather than a mere violation of art, **(9) ______** he transformed Cattelan’s work and put it back on display.

The Noh drama lasted only around 30 minutes, as museum workers installed a second banana **(10) ______** *(restore)* Cattelan’s work. The museum won’t pursue Noh for any damages, according to Korean news outlets. *Comedian* is the centerpiece of a large exhibition of Cattelan’s art at the Leeum that’s going on through July. The banana art is even printed on a special coffee cup to announce the approach of the 38 works.

> 注：本题按图片人工/视觉校对识别，未调用 GLM-OCR。

本篇错题：**2、3、5、6、8、9、10**，共 7 题。

### 【你的答案】

2. more simple（图片疑似；核心错误为未用标准比较级）
3. skipped
5. What
6. and
8. which
9. so
10. restoring

### 【正确答案】

2. simpler
3. had skipped
5. It
6. which
8. whether / if
9. because / as / since
10. to restore

### 【错误原因分析】

本篇主要问题集中在：

1. **形容词比较级**：`simple` 的标准比较级是 **simpler**，常见表达为 `in simpler terms`。
2. **过去完成时**：第 3 题表示“没吃早饭”发生在“提醒他/让他想起”之前，要用 **had skipped**。
3. **形式主语句型**：`It takes sb. some time to do sth.` 中必须用形式主语 **It**。
4. **非限制性定语从句**：第 6 题先行词是 KBS，空格后缺主语，应填 **which**。
5. **宾语从句连接词**：第 8 题表达“是否”，应用 **whether / if**。
6. **原因状语从句**：第 9 题后文解释原因，应填 **because / as / since**，不是 `so`。
7. **不定式作目的状语**：第 10 题表示“为了恢复作品”，应用 **to restore**。

## 📝 错题 #3.1

### 【题目原文】

However, it spoke to Noh Hyun-soo in **(2) ______** *(simple)* terms, reminding him that he **(3) ______** *(skip)* breakfast that morning.

### 【你的答案】

more simple（图片疑似；核心错误为未用标准比较级）

### 【正确答案】

simpler

### 【错误原因分析】

这里是固定且自然的表达：

$$
\text{in simpler terms}
$$

意思是“用更简单的话来说”。`simple` 是双音节词，但比较级常用 **simpler**，比 `more simple` 更标准、更符合考试答案。

### 【涉及知识点】

- 形容词比较级
- 固定表达 `in simpler terms`

### 【解题思路】

看到空格后面是 **terms**，应联想到表达：

$$
\text{in simple/simpler terms}
$$

句中含有从艺术意义转向“更简单的饥饿需求”的意思，因此填比较级 **simpler**。

## 📝 错题 #3.6
