## 📝 错题 #1

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:必修/选修教材 Unit 2 Roads to Education 词汇与语法专项
**知识点**:非谓语动词(过去分词/现在分词作定语或状语), 词性转换与语境搭配, 固定短语与介词搭配, 被动语态与省略结构

### 【题目原文】
用方框中所给单词的适当形式填空。
refusal, point, set, personally, expelling, proposal, positive, dealing, questioned, quoted, punctuated

1. The boss required me to work at home and pay close attention to the deadlines ______ by clients.
2. The mayor is ______ by the newspaper as saying that people will be banned from eating in the underground next month.
3. The judge ruled that the man found guilty of ______ drugs with the guy from the gangster should be put behind bars for three decades.
4. The silence was suddenly ______ by laughter of the audience in the theatre due to the performer's joke, which was quite unexpected and amusing.
5. When threatened, some rattlesnakes hiss loudly ______ air from the lung. Such a distinctive whirling sound is made by vibrating its tail rattle.
6. When ______ by the police, you have the right to remain silent. If you do decide to speak to the police, the statements can be used against you in a court of law.
7. In 1916, a woman's group named the Woman's International Bowling Congress (WIBC) told the world that it would make a(n) ______ of showing that the game was fine for both men and women.
8. Teenagers tend not to accept parents' ______ to admit their mistakes, especially when what their parents have done has proved to be wrong.
9. Anyone testing ______ for COVID-19, whether or not they are fully vaccinated, must be quarantined and follow the guidance of a medical provider or contact tracer.
10. When you are making a speech, make sure that you stand up straight, keep your head up and look straight at your audience to make them feel as if you were talking to them personally.

### 【你的答案】
> 1. set  2. positive  3. punctuated  4. expelling  5. quoted  6. questioned  7. point  8. refusal  9. proposal  10. personally

### 【正确答案】
**1. set  2. quoted  3. dealing  4. punctuated  5. expelling  6. questioned  7. point  8. refusal  9. positive  10. personally**

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **被动语态识别错误**:第2题误填 positive(形容词),应为 quoted(过去分词表被动),be quoted by 表示"被......引用"。
2. **固定搭配不熟**:第3题 guilty of 后必须接动名词,deal drugs(贩毒),故填 dealing;第9题 test positive for(检测呈阳性),positive 为形容词作补语,非 proposal。
3. **语境逻辑判断失误**:第5题 When questioned by the police(被警察讯问时),与 quoted(被引用)语义不符。
4. **干扰项排除**:第7题 make a point of doing sth.(特意做某事),proposal 为干扰项。

### 【解题思路】
本题为高中英语常见的"选词填空+词形转换"题型,需结合句意、语法结构(尤其是非谓语动词和固定搭配)进行综合判断。
1. **set**:考查过去分词作后置定语。deadlines(截止日期)与 set(设定)为被动关系,故用 set 的过去分词 set(set 的过去分词与原形同形)。
2. **quoted**:考查过去分词作表语/被动语态。be quoted by... 表示"被......引用/报道",符合语境"市长被报纸引用称......"。
3. **dealing**:考查固定搭配 guilty of doing sth.(犯有......罪/做错事)。deal drugs 意为"贩毒",guilty of 后必须接动名词,故填 dealing。
4. **punctuated**:考查过去分词作后置定语/被动语态。silence(沉默)与 punctuate(打断、点缀)为被动关系,be punctuated by 表示"被......打破/点缀",符合"沉默突然被笑声打断"的语境。
5. **expelling**:考查现在分词作伴随/方式状语。蛇通过肺部主动将空气"排出"(expel air),逻辑主语 rattlesnakes 与 expel 为主动关系,故用 expelling。
6. **questioned**:考查状语从句省略结构。When questioned by the police 为完整句 When (you are) questioned by the police 的省略形式,表示"当被警察讯问时",需用过去分词表被动。
7. **point**:考查固定搭配 make a point of doing sth. = "特意做某事;郑重其事地做某事"。a(n) 后接名词,故填 point。
8. **refusal**:考查名词作定语/宾语。parents' 后接名词,refusal to do sth. 表示"拒绝做某事",结合语境指青少年往往不接受父母的强硬态度/拒绝,故填 refusal。
9. **positive**:考查固定搭配 test positive for...(检测呈阳性)。positive 为形容词作结果状语/补语,描述检测后的状态,不可改为副词。
10. **personally**:考查副词修饰动词。修饰动词 talking,表示"亲自、面对面地",故用副词 personally。
(注:方框中的 proposal 为干扰项,本题未使用。)

## 📝 错题 #2

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:高中英语词汇运用 / 语境选词填空
**知识点**:词义辨析, 语境逻辑推理, 词性判断与语法形式, 固定搭配与习惯用法

### 【题目原文】
Exercise 2 单句生词填空(B套)

词库:influential cleared pursuit reflects inspiration drew identifiable uniform employed settings mastered

1. Experts have been emphasizing that if the jungle is totally ______ to plant crops, there will be no means of putting fertility back into the soil.
2. Peking Opera is the most ______ and representative of all operas in China and is widely regarded as a full expression of Chinese culture.
3. Objects at rest tend to remain at rest, while moving ones continue to move at a(n) ______ speed in a straight line unless acted upon by an outside force.
4. The most important speaking skill lies particularly in deciding how much gesturing to be ______, and in making sure that your gestures are natural.
5. Scott's last journey, completed as he lay in a tent dying of cold and hunger, caught the world's imagination, and a film made in his honor then ______ crowds.
6. Since most TV "action-adventure" dramas occur in urban ______, the fear they inspire may contribute to the current escape of the middle class from our cities.
7. The cartoon vividly ______ a common phenomenon in reality; many people are becoming increasingly indulged in the virtual world presented by smartphones.
8. Lance has been the champion of the Tour de France for a record 7 times since he was diagnosed with cancer, which has made him a symbol of hope and a source of ______.
9. In big cities, styles of dress are particularly important with regard to self-presentation. Different groups often use clearly ______ styles of clothes so that they can be easily recognized.
10. It is quite doubtful whether it is necessary to build so many 3D cinemas, since 2D films still share the majority of China's movie market. It is likely that the over-______ of 3D films can be a waste of money.

### 【你的答案】
> 1. mastered  2. influential  3. cleared  4. employed  5. drew  6. settings  7. reflects  8. inspiration  9. identifiable  10. pursuit

### 【正确答案】
**1. cleared  2. influential  3. uniform  4. employed  5. drew  6. settings  7. reflects  8. inspiration  9. identifiable  10. pursuit**

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **词性混淆**:如 inspire(动词)与 inspiration(名词)不分,settings(名词复数)与 identifiable(形容词)误用。学生易忽略空格前后的语法结构提示,直接按中文意思选词。
2. **语法形式遗漏**:未注意被动语态(如 employed, mastered)、时态(如 drew)、主谓一致(如 reflects)。选词填空常设语法陷阱,需先分析句子结构再选词。
3. **语境逻辑偏差**:忽视固定搭配与习惯用法(如 draw crowds, source of inspiration, uniform speed),仅凭局部词汇直译导致偏差。
4. **干扰项排除不力**:词库中 cleared、settings 为典型干扰项。cleared 虽可接 land/jungle,但 mastered 更契合"彻底掌控"的学术语境;settings 为名词复数,无法作定语修饰 styles,需通过词性直接排除。

### 【解题思路】
【逐题解析】
1. **cleared**:语境指"清除丛林以开垦农田"。clear the jungle 意为"清理丛林(砍伐植被)",此处为被动语态 be cleared to do sth.。
2. **influential**:修饰 Peking Opera,与 representative 并列作前置定语。influential 意为"有影响力的",符合京剧作为中国文化代表的地位。
3. **uniform**:结合物理常识与语境"匀速直线运动",uniform speed 为固定搭配,意为"匀速"。
4. **employed**:考查被动语态 to be employed。employ 在此处意为"使用、采用",指手势在演讲中的运用。
5. **drew**:考查动词时态。电影上映吸引观众为过去发生的动作,用一般过去时 drew。draw crowds 为固定搭配。
6. **inspire**:考查定语从句谓语动词。TV dramas 为复数主语,且描述客观事实,用一般现在时原形 inspire。inspire fear 意为"引发恐惧"。
7. **reflects**:考查主句谓语动词。主语 The cartoon 为单数,描述客观现象用一般现在时 reflects。reflect 意为"反映",符合漫画与现实的关系。
8. **inspiration**:考查名词形式。source of ______ 需填名词,inspiration 意为"灵感、鼓舞",与 hope 并列,符合语境。
9. **identifiable**:考查形容词作定语。identifiable styles 意为"可识别的/有辨识度的"服装风格,符合"不同群体通过着装区分"的语境。
10. **pursuit**:考查名词。over-______ 需填名词,pursuit 意为"追求"。注:原题 OCR 识别为 purush 属拼写错误,根据词库及语境应修正为 pursuit。over-pursuit 指"过度追求"。

【解题步骤】
1 通读全句,划出空格前后的关键词(如冠词、介词、并列连词、从句引导词等);
2 分析空格在句中的语法成分,确定所需词性(名/动/形/副);
3 结合固定搭配、逻辑关系(因果/转折/并列)及上下文线索,从词库中筛选匹配项;
4 检查语法形式(时态、语态、主谓一致、单复数、派生词性)是否完全正确;
5 将剩余干扰项(如 settings、cleared)代入验证,排除语义或语法冲突的选项。

## 📝 错题 #3

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:中英互译与核心语法应用
**知识点**:Despite/Although 引导的让步状语, Convert A into B 固定搭配, Expel sb from school 动词用法, Have the potential to do 句型, Point at 指向, Deal with 涉及/处理, Judging from 固定搭配, Have an effect on 产生...影响, 非谓语动词作主语/状语, 定语从句与被动语态

### 【题目原文】
Exercise 3 中英互译(A套)
1. 虽然那位保安在农场工作,但他从先前的老师那儿借来复习资料,在空闲时间坚持学习。(despite)
2. 将煤转化为气体会产生大量的碳排放,但目前人们还无法完全放弃使用它。(convert... into...)
3. 由于你这学期不会来上课,我拒绝给你及格分。我甚至可以把你从学校开除。(expel)
4. 彼得老师相信,通过帮助学生组建一个俱乐部,他们有潜力去表现得更好。(behave)
5. 既然课前演讲很简短,你说话时,就别总是指着稿子了。(point)
6. 除了一些标点符号的不当使用,你的文章挺好,因为涉及一个很有教育意义的话题。(deal)
7. 从观众的鼓掌来判断,演奏音乐来帮助演员表达感情的做法效果挺好。(judge)

> 1. Despite the fact that that policeman is working on the farm, he is still studying the review materials that borrowed from his prvious teachers in the spare time.

> 10. 这个摇滚乐队将一个工作室改造成了一个客厅,并且决定退掉任何不必要的看访,仅仅探索音乐世界并一起影响他人。

### 【正确答案】
**1. Despite working on the farm as a security guard, he borrowed review materials from his former teachers and persisted in studying in his spare time.**
**2. Converting coal into gas produces a large amount of carbon emissions, but people are still unable to completely give up using it.**

**4. Teacher Peter believes that by helping students form a club, they have the potential to behave better.**

**7. Judging from the audience's applause, playing music to help actors express their emotions has proved quite effective.**
**8. This 60-year-old tutor has had a positive effect on his graduate students and was finally awarded the title of "Teacher of the Year" by Fudan University.**

**10. 这支摇滚乐队已将工作室改造成客厅,并决定谢绝一切不必要的拜访,只为共同探索音乐世界、寻找灵感。**

### 【逐题错误分析】

**第1题**：① Despite the fact that... 语法正确但啰嗦，应改为 Despite + 动名词；② policeman（警察）→ security guard（保安）；③ that borrowed → borrowed（被动关系）；④ previous 拼写错误；⑤ is still studying → persisted in studying（"坚持学习"）。

**第2题**：① Coal is converted into air 有两个谓语，应改为动名词 Converting coal into gas；② air（空气）→ gas（气体）；③ large of → a large amount of；④ emisson 拼写错误；⑤ give up to use → give up using。

**第3题**：① Due to 后误接句子 → Since/Because + 句子；② hardly came → won't be attending；③ scores → a passing grade（及格分）；④ I even could → I could even（even 位置）；⑤ 逗号粘连，应改为句号。

**第4题**：① Peter teacher → Teacher Peter（称呼语序）；② believed → believes（一般性观点）；③ 擅自添加 for difficult students；④ have potential → have the potential（缺冠词）。

**第5题**：① after classing → pre-class；② whe → while（拼写）；③ don't away point → don't always point（语序）；④ novles → notes（拼写+词义）。

**第6题**：① Apart from 后误接句子 → the improper use of...（名词短语）；② punctuations → punctuation marks（不可数）；③ uncorrectly → improper（形容词修饰 use）；④ novel → essay；⑤ refers to → deals with（涉及话题）。

**第7题**：① audiences' claps → audience's applause（正式表达）；② perform emotions → express emotions；③ have → has（动名词作主语，谓语用单数）。

**第8题**：① 60-year-ld → 60-year-old（拼写）；② teachers → tutor（主谓一致+词义）；③ poisitive → positive（拼写）；④ their searchers → his graduate students（词义）；⑤ is awarded → was awarded（时态）；⑥ in → by（被动语态的动作发出者）。

**第9题**：① 野炊 → campus（校园）；② 设计 → directed（执导）；③ 短片 → 短视频；④ 展出 → 播出（broadcast）；⑤ 漏译 soon（不久）。

**第10题**：① 退掉 → 谢绝（decline）；② 看访 → 拜访（笔误）；③ 仅仅 → 只为（just to 表目的）；④ seek → 寻找（非影响）；⑤ inspiration → 灵感（非他人）。

### 【解题思路】
【逐题解析】
1. **考点**:Despite + 名词/动名词。学生易误用 despite 后接完整句子(应改为 Although/Though)。正确结构:Despite doing sth / Despite the fact that...。注意"坚持学习"译为 persist in / keep on studying。
2. **考点**:动名词作主语。将煤转化为气体 → Converting coal into gas。注意"碳排放"为 carbon emissions,不可直译为 emission of coal。
3. **考点**:拒绝做某事 refuse to do sth;开除 expel sb from school。时态需用一般将来时或现在进行时表将来(won't be attending)。
4. **考点**:have the potential to do sth(有潜力做某事);behave 在此处意为"表现/举止得体",非"行为"。
5. **考点**:point at 指向。OCR 误识别为 novels,实际应为 notes(笔记/讲稿)。注意祈使句否定形式 don't always point at...
6. **考点**:deal with 在此处意为"涉及/讨论(话题)",而非"处理"。文章应为 essay/article,非 novel(小说)。
7. **考点**:Judging from... 为固定搭配,作独立成分,不随主语变化。效果挺好 → has proved effective / has a good effect。
8. **考点**:have an effect on sb(对...产生影响);award sb the title of...(授予...头衔)。OCR 严重失真:"searchers"应为 students,"Teacher of the fear"应为 Teacher of the Year,"Hudan"应为 Fudan。
9. **考点**:过去分词 set on campus 作后置定语修饰 videos;which 引导非限制性定语从句,指代前面整件事或 videos,谓语用单数/复数视语境而定(此处指 videos 将被播出,用 will be broadcast)。
10. **考点**:convert A into B;decline visits(谢绝拜访);seek inspiration(寻找灵感)。注意现在完成时 has converted/has decided 的并列结构。

## 📝 错题 #5

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：高中英语翻译与长难句专项训练
**知识点**：中英互译技巧, 核心动词短语与固定搭配, 定语从句与非谓语动词, 重点句型结构 (so...that, the reason...is that, whether引导的主语从句), 语境逻辑与长难句拆分

### 【题目原文】

### 【逐题错误分析】

**第1题**：① oxygen carbon → carbon dioxide（二氧化碳）；② take → take in（吸收）；③ makes a great difference on → playing a key role in dealing with（起关键作用）；④ and it makes... → 改用现在分词作结果状语

**第2题**：① During the class of Internet → when we had online lessons（网课）；② take charge of the resposibility → Taking on the responsibility（承担）；③ watching children have classes → encouraging children to attend classes（教促孩子上课）；④ looking forward the early ends of the . → looking forward to the early end of the pandemic（漏to，句子不完整）

**第3题**：① illed → fell ill（生病）；② symptons → symptoms（拼写）；③ is taking fade → are fading away（fade away = 消失，不能用take）；④ luckily → Fortunately（正式）

**第4题**：① As speaking the conflict of workroom → When speaking of office conflicts（谈及）；② reflect themselves → reflect on themselves（反思自己）；③ talk with their workmates → communicate with their colleagues（同事）；④ on time → in time（及时，≠准时）

**第5题**：① personal runner → self-employed person（个体经营者，runner=跑步者）；② make a lot of time → have more leisure time（有休闲时间）；③ purse → pursue（追求，purse=钱包）

**第6题**：① Chinese drawer's → painted by this master of Chinese traditional painting（中国画大师所绘就的）；② so lively, so that → so vivid that（活灵活现=vivid，so...that中间不加逗号）；③ are long to buy → long to buy（long作动词，不需要are）；④ from him → of his（他的作品）

**第7题**：① smelling garden → fragrant garden（芳香四溢的）；② I minded in this childhood memory like a new one → keep this childhood memory fresh in my mind（记忆犹新，固定表达）

**第8题**：① There is a huge impact whether set a suitable aim on → Whether you can set a reasonable goal has a huge impact on（Whether引导主语从句，不能拆成There be句型）；② suitable thinking → reasonable assessment（评估）；③ person skills → current skill level（当前技能水平）

**第9题**：① 研究报告 → research team（研究团队）；② 限制胃病的原始病毒 → 对胃癌原始疗法的依赖（tight hold of the original cure for stomach cancer）；③ 没有找到...的方法 → 仍未摆脱（shake off=摆脱）

**第10题**：① 代替 → 而不是（Instead of表转折）；② 有好工资的工作 → 高薪工作（well-paid job）；③ 帮助他们能找到在他们生活中的希望 → 重新燃起对生活的希望（find their new hopes in their lives）

### 【解题思路】
【逐题解析与翻译策略】
1. 【考点】take in（吸收）；play a key/vital role in (doing) sth.（起关键作用）。注意“应对全球变暖”用 dealing with/coping with global warming。后半句用现在分词作伴随/结果状语更符合英语表达习惯。
2. 【考点】take on the responsibility of doing sth.（承担做某事的责任）；look forward to (doing) sth.（期盼）。前半句可用现在分词作状语，或独立主句。注意 pandemic（疫情）的拼写。
3. 【考点】fall ill（生病）；a series of（一系列）；fade away/disappear（消失）。注意时态：昨天用过去时，现在用现在进行时。
4. 【考点】speak of/about（谈及）；reflect on oneself（反思自己）；communicate with（与...沟通）。suggest后接宾语从句用虚拟语气（should）do，should可省略。
5. 【考点】the reason why... is that...（...的原因是...）；self-employed（个体经营者）；leisure time（休闲时间）；pursue one's dream（追求梦想）。注意主从句时态一致。
6. 【考点】so...that...（如此...以至于...）；vivid/lifelike（活灵活现）；long to do sth.（渴望做某事）。定语“中国画大师所绘就的”用过去分词 painted by... 作后置定语。
7. 【考点】keep sth. fresh in one's mind（记忆犹新）；fragrant（芳香四溢的）；childhood memory。注意中英文语序调整，中文先时间状语，英文可前置或后置。
8. 【考点】Whether引导的主语从句（能否...）；have an impact on（对...有影响）；effectiveness（效果）；make an assessment of（评估）。注意主谓一致，Whether从句整体作主语，谓语用单数 has。
9. 【考点】shake off（摆脱）；tight hold of（紧紧抓住/依赖）；to this day（至今）；give up halfway（半途而废）。注意主谓一致及否定完成时态。
10. 【考点】Instead of doing（而不是做...）；volunteer to do（志愿做...）；lacking...作后置定语修饰students；find new hope（找到新希望）。注意长句拆分，先译主干再译修饰成分。
