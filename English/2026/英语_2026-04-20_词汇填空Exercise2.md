## 📝 错题 #8

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：高中英语词汇运用/词义辨析与语境填空
**知识点**：词义辨析, 固定搭配与短语, 语境逻辑推理, 词性判断

### 【题目原文】
**Exercise 2 单句生词填空（B套）**

**备选词汇**：failure, late, receiving, enormous, competitive, seriously, attaining, accomplishments, false, continuous, firmly

1. As long as you are determined to do it well and make _______ efforts, you are sure to succeed.
2. Memorial Day is about honoring the _______ soldiers who made the ultimate sacrifice for their country.
3. Health food became popular when people began to take their physical well-being more _______.
4. Experts say when it comes to a sudden heart _______, the single best chance for survival is having someone nearby step in and do CPR quickly.
5. Try _______ new skills and hobbies so that you'll start to be more confident of your abilities.
6. He had a very individualistic style and his _______ have led to his being revered by all artists devoted to the ideals of the literati tradition.
7. Try to send a positive message through your body language in the interview. Shake hands _______, for example, but only if a hand is offered to you first.
8. Dental plaque（牙菌斑） buildup on _______ teeth can lead to bone loss, bad breath, and stomatitis, inflammation of the soft tissue lining inside the mouth.
9. After _______ his three-year high school education, Zhang Hua was admitted to Fudan University.
10. South Korea has long been famous for its _______ education system. But this year, for the 700,000 high-school students in the country, high-school studies have become even more intense due to the new college admission policy.

### 【选项】
- A. failure
- B. late
- C. receiving
- D. enormous
- E. competitive
- F. seriously
- G. attaining
- H. accomplishments
- I. false
- J. continuous
- K. firmly

### 【你的答案】
1. enormous · 2. continous · 3. seriously · 4. failure · 5. firmly · 6. accomplishments · 7. attaining · 8. false · 9. receiving · 10. competitive

### 【正确答案】
1. **continuous** · 2. **late** · 3. **seriously** · 4. **failure** · 5. **attaining** · 6. **accomplishments** · 7. **firmly** · 8. **false** · 9. **receiving** · 10. **competitive**

### 【答题统计】
**6/10 正确** -- 错了 4 道(第 1、2、5、7 题)

### 【正确答案】
**1. continuous  2. late  3. seriously  4. failure  5. attaining  6. accomplishments  7. firmly  8. false  9. receiving  10. competitive**

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **词义多义性混淆**：如late（迟到的/已故的）、false（假的/人造的）等多义词未结合语境精准定位；failure（名词：故障/衰竭）与fail（动词：失败）词性混淆。
2. **固定搭配不熟**：如take seriously（认真对待）、heart failure（心脏骤停）、false teeth（假牙）等高频短语未形成条件反射，导致凭主观语感误选。
3. **词性判断失误**：未准确分析空格在句中的语法功能。例如第3题需副词seriously修饰动词take；第5题try后接动名词attaining作宾语；第9题介词after后需接动名词receiving。词性误用是常见失分点。
4. **语境逻辑与常识缺失**：第2题Memorial Day提示“纪念/牺牲”，应选late（已故的）而非时间副词；第10题韩国教育“内卷/竞争激烈”的常识语境需结合competitive理解。未充分利用上下文线索和背景知识是主要问题。

### 【解题思路】
本题为语境词汇填空，需结合句意、语法结构及固定搭配进行精准匹配。备选词中“enormous”为干扰项。

1. **continuous**：修饰名词efforts，表示“持续的”。make continuous efforts（持续努力）为高频搭配，符合“只要坚持努力就一定能成功”的逻辑。
2. **late**：修饰soldiers。此处late作形容词意为“已故的、逝去的”，late soldiers指“阵亡将士/已故士兵”，完美契合Memorial Day（阵亡将士纪念日）的语境。
3. **seriously**：修饰动词take，构成固定搭配take sth. seriously（认真对待某事）。副词修饰动词，符合语法要求。
4. **failure**：heart failure为固定医学术语，意为“心力衰竭/心脏骤停”。此处考查学科交叉词汇及固定搭配。
5. **attaining**：try doing sth. 表示“尝试做某事”。attaining（获得、达成、掌握）作动名词作try的宾语，符合语境“尝试掌握新技能”。（注：原题OCR文本第5句误植为firmly，根据词库及句意应填attaining，第7句才填firmly）
6. **accomplishments**：作主语，指“成就、功绩”。his accomplishments（他的成就）与后文“被艺术家们敬仰”形成因果逻辑。
7. **firmly**：修饰动词短语shake hands，表示“坚定地、有力地”。shake hands firmly（有力地握手）符合面试礼仪语境，ad. 坚固地为其词性标注及中文释义。
8. **false**：false teeth为固定搭配，意为“假牙”。false在此表示“人造的、非天然的”，区别于fake（仿冒的）。
9. **receiving**：after为介词，后接动名词作宾语。receiving his education（接受教育）符合语境。
10. **competitive**：修饰education system，表示“竞争激烈的”。competitive education system（竞争激烈的教育体系）符合韩国教育现状的描述，与后文“学习强度加大”形成呼应。
