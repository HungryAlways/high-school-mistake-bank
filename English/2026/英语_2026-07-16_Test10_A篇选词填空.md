# Test 10 A篇选词填空

## 题目要求

Complete the following passage by using the words in the box. Each word can only be used once. Note that there is one word more than you need.

使用方框中的单词完成短文。每个单词只能使用一次，并且有一个多余选项。

## 选项

A. established  
B. cultural  
C. freely  
D. introduce  
E. official  
F. release  
G. extremely  
H. attraction  
I. drew  
J. unexpectedly  
K. mystery

## 完整原文

A large-scale cultural exploration program *National Treasure* made its first show on CCTV on Sunday night. In the first episode, the Palace Museum in Beijing showcased three treasures.

As a **(1) ______** variety show, the *National Treasure* aims to showcase the background stories of national treasures and **(2) ______** various art forms. In the show, “national treasure keepers” acted by famous and common people will present treasures, telling their stories with the collections and interpreting the historical **(3) ______**. The show aims to inspire public interest in the ancient Chinese civilization and make the national treasures “come alive”.

This is not the first time for the Palace Museum in Beijing to be **(4) ______** welcomed online in China, thanks to its efforts in cultural products and self-promotion in recent years.

Last year, the museum **(5) ______** so much attention because of the huge success of the three-episode TV documentary, *Masters in the Forbidden City* and a movie with the same name. Over the last few years, the museum has **(6) ______** 495 signposts and 1,400 new chairs have been provided. In addition, the Palace Museum opened a(n) **(7) ______** online store on e-commerce platform Taobao, selling related products. It also started their self-promotion through new media, publishing articles to promote the culture of the museum. It **(8) ______** swept the Internet by its humorous style and interesting content. At the same time, the Palace Museum has published several mobile apps, one of which saw over 200,000 downloads just two weeks after its **(9) ______** in 2013. Its self-promotion has achieved great results. In 2012, the museum saw 15 million visitors. And it received 16 million visitors last year.

The museum is also a popular **(10) ______** among foreign leaders. For example, US President Donald Trump and his wife visited the Palace Museum on Nov 8, 2017.

## 错误小题

第 **1、4、7、8、9、10** 空，共6处。

## 逐项错误分析

### 第1空：official → cultural

原句：`As a ______ variety show...`

空格需要形容词说明节目的类别。《国家宝藏》围绕文物、历史和中华文化展开，正确搭配是 **a cultural variety show**。`official` 虽然也是形容词，但“官方综艺节目”不符合本文对节目性质的描述。

**错误类型：** 词义辨析错误、上下文判断错误。

### 第4空：unexpectedly → extremely

原句：`to be ______ welcomed online`

这里强调故宫在网上“非常受欢迎”，需要程度副词 **extremely**。`unexpectedly` 表示“出乎意料地”，不符合本句重点。

**错误类型：** 副词词义辨析错误、上下文逻辑错误。

### 第7空：cultural → official

原句：`opened a(n) ______ online store`

正确搭配是 **an official online store**，即“官方网上商店”。`cultural online store` 虽然形式上可作定语，但不能准确表达故宫在淘宝开设官方商店的语义。

**错误类型：** 形容词辨析错误、上下文判断错误。

### 第8空：freely → unexpectedly

原句：`It ______ swept the Internet...`

`sweep the Internet` 表示“席卷网络、迅速走红”。故宫新媒体内容以幽默风格走红，带有出乎意料的意味，应填 **unexpectedly**。`freely swept the Internet` 搭配不成立。

**错误类型：** 副词搭配错误、单词词义记忆错误。

### 第9空：attraction → release

原句：`two weeks after its ______ in 2013`

应用在“发布两周后”获得超过20万次下载，正确表达是 **after its release**。`attraction` 表示“吸引力、景点”，不符合时间逻辑。

**错误类型：** 词义辨析错误、上下文逻辑错误、固定表达不熟悉。

### 第10空：release → attraction

原句：`a popular ______ among foreign leaders`

冠词 `a` 和形容词 `popular` 后面需要名词。结合博物馆语境，应为 **a popular attraction**。`release` 表示“发布、发行”，不能表示受外国领导人欢迎的参观地点。

**错误类型：** 固定搭配错误、词义辨析错误。

## 综合错误原因

1. 同词性选项之间的语义匹配不准确，如 `official/cultural`、`release/attraction`。
2. 三个副词没有结合语境和搭配区分：`extremely welcomed`、`unexpectedly swept the Internet`，而 `freely` 为多余项。
3. 未充分利用“一词只能使用一次”进行全局复核，导致部分选项连锁错位。

## 正确解题思路

1. 先给选项标注词性。
2. 根据空格前后结构判断所需词性。
3. 优先识别固定搭配：`draw attention`、`official online store`、`after its release`、`a popular attraction`。
4. 对同词性的副词和形容词放回完整句子比较语义。
5. 完成后检查每个词是否只使用一次，并通读全文复核。

## 易错提醒

- `extremely` 强调程度；`unexpectedly` 强调结果出乎意料。
- `official online store` 表示“官方网上商店”。
- `draw attention` 表示“引起关注”。
- `after its release` 表示“在它发布之后”。
- `attraction` 可表示“景点”或“有吸引力的事物”。
- 十一选十做完后必须检查选项是否重复或错位。
