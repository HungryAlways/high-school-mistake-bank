# 英语阅读理解 Test 20 - Part C（渔船躲避巡逻艇搜查）（2026-07-12）

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：阅读理解（记叙文·情节推理）

---

## 【题目原文】（完整原文，可用来重做）

### C

Jack looked up and saw in the distance red and green navigation lights coming towards them and knew it must be a naval *patrol*(巡逻) boat because of its speed. As the humming of the boat's powerful engines drew closer, Jack bent down.

Jack then let out a frightened cry as the brilliant beam of a searchlight broke the darkness and swept the fishing boat from head to tail. By its reflected light, Jack could clearly see the similar looks on the faces of other bent ones.

Jack knew that if the patrol boat came close enough, its men would surely see the people hidden in the fishing boat. To collect himself, he leaned his head a little so that he could see Derek standing in the head of the boat, holding the *tiller*(舵柄). Derek managed to wave back at the patrol boat as if he had not a care in the world. How imperturbable he was!

Someone on board the patrol boat switched on a loud speaker and a voice, made harsh and thin by its horn, demanded, "Where are you bound?"

Derek shrugged and shouted back, "Tell me where the fish run and I'll tell you where I am bound." The noise of idling engines drowned his voice.

"Say again!" barked the speaker.

"Anywhere where there's fish!" Derek shouted back. There was a pause. The boats drifted closer to each other.

"Why aren't you showing navigation lights?"

Jack watched as Derek pretended to look puzzled and leant out over the side as if to see what they said were true. "They must have blown out," he apologized with a shrug. "I'll light them again as soon as you stop rocking me about like this."

Aboard the patrol boat, the captain was wondering if it would be worthwhile searching this old fishing boat for stowaways as how many breathing souls could such a shabby boat hide? The radio suddenly cackled. Abruptly, without another word being exchanged, the light went out and the patrol boat started its engines, speeding off into the dark, leaving the fishing boat floating about in the water.

## 【题目】

**1. Jack let out a scared cry because he ________**
A. saw something in the sea
B. was afraid of being discovered
C. was blinded by the bright lights
D. was scared by the faces of companions.

**2. The patrol boat captain DIDN'T search the fishing boat as he ______.**
A. didn't find it worthwhile
B. looked down upon the shabby old fishing boat
C. trusted Derek very much
D. was called to another place via the radio

**3. The underlined word "*imperturbable*" in the passage is closest in meaning to ______.**
A. anxious
B. nervous
C. calm
D. sociable

**4. Which of the following statements is true according to the passage?**
A. Derek didn't realize that Jack and others hid in the fishing boat.
B. The captain was suspicious of what Derek told him.
C. The patrol boat approached the fishing boat to inspect illegal goods.
D. Derek succeeded in covering up for Jack and others on the boat.

## 错题详解

### 第2题（错） B → 正确 D. was called to another place via the radio
- 原文结尾：`the captain was wondering **if it would be worthwhile** searching this old fishing boat... **The radio suddenly cackled.** Abruptly, without another word being exchanged, the light went out and the patrol boat started its engines, speeding off`
- **关键推理**：船长只是"在**犹豫**值不值得搜(wondering if worthwhile)"——**还没做决定**；紧接着"**无线电突然响了(radio cackled)**"，然后**立刻二话不说开船离开**。让他放弃搜船、马上驶离的**直接原因是无线电呼叫(D)**。
- ⚠️ 陷阱 A/B：worthwhile 只是船长**心里还在盘算的状态**，不是最终原因；B"看不起(look down upon)"原文无此意（原文是 shabby「破旧」+ wondering worthwhile，不是轻视）。
- 📌 教训：题干问"DIDN'T search 的原因"，要找**直接触发离开的事件**（radio cackled → sped off），别被前半句 worthwhile 这个"未完成的想法"带偏。

### 第4题（错） C → 正确 D. Derek succeeded in covering up for Jack and others
- 全文结局：Derek 装傻应付盘问（哪有鱼去哪、灯坏了），最终巡逻艇没搜船就离开 → Derek **成功替 Jack 等偷渡者打掩护**。选 D。
- C（巡逻艇是来"检查非法货物 inspect illegal goods"）——原文只说搜 stowaways（偷渡者/藏匿的人），**没提 illegal goods**，无中生有。
- A（Derek 不知道有人藏船里）——错，Derek 明显在帮忙打掩护，知情。
- B（船长怀疑 Derek 说的话）——原文没有船长起疑的描写，他是"犹豫值不值得搜"，最后被无线电叫走，谈不上"suspicious"。

## 对题回顾
- **第1题** → B：Jack 吓得叫出声，因探照灯扫过、怕船上藏的人被发现（下文 "if the patrol boat came close enough, its men would surely see the people hidden"）。
- **第3题** → C：imperturbable（沉着冷静的）≈ calm。上文 "waved back...as if he had not a care in the world"（若无其事地挥手）→ 镇定。anxious/nervous 相反，sociable（善交际）无关。

## 错误原因分析
1. **第2题**：被 "worthwhile" 这个词吸引（B/A 都围绕它设陷阱），忽略了真正触发船长离开的事件"radio cackled"。**因果题要找直接触发的动作/事件，而非人物"还在犹豫"的心理**。
2. **第4题**：选了原文没提到的信息（illegal goods 无中生有）。**"which is true"题要逐项回原文核对，选项里出现原文未提及的新信息即可排除**。

## 知识点
- 记叙文因果推理题：区分"人物心理活动(wondering, 未决定)"与"真正触发结果的事件(radio cackled → left)"
- "无中生有"干扰项识别：illegal goods、suspicious 等原文未提及的信息
- 词义题结合上下文：imperturbable = calm（had not a care in the world 佐证）
- stowaways（偷渡者）≠ illegal goods（非法货物）

## 举一反三
1. 因果题：文中"他正想着要不要休息，这时电话响了，他立刻出门"——他出门的直接原因是？（电话，而非"想休息"）
2. 无中生有辨析：which is true 题，选项含原文未出现的名词/信息 → 排除
3. 词义题：imperturbable / composed / unruffled 都表"沉着"；anxious / nervous / restless 表"焦虑"
