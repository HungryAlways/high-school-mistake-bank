## 📝 错题 #1

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:Unit 3 Progress? / 核心词汇与语境填空
**知识点**:固定搭配、词性转换、语境辨析

### 【题目原文】
【词库】figures, threaten, capital, rated, continual, familiarize, populated, expertise, race, alarm, imaginary

1. Some marathon runners may suffer from kidney injury after finishing the **race**.
2. Lack of fear of nature can partly account for the ______ outbreak of natural disasters across the world.
3. Many parents of preschoolers struggle with their children's fears of ______ creatures.
4. The following guidelines and suggestions will ______ you with active reading techniques.
5. When it comes to the causes of stress, remember that your brain comes with a(n) ______ system for your protection.
6. Not only is the Hongkong Ocean Park the largest in Asia, but it is also ______ as one of the most spectacular oceanariums in the world.
7. The new strategy involves offering tech start-ups free rent, reduced tax rates, easier visa policies, and access to government-backed venture ______.
8. According to research conducted by Engma HR Co. LTD, 55.6 percent of HR professionals say they value a graduate's abilities over their specific area of ______.
9. This project was intended to save one of these dying regions - the remote and sparsely ______ regions - where the way of life had declined to a point of desperation.
10. Among the Committee's 51 members are top-university presidents, scholars, and business executives, as well as distinguished ______ from diplomacy, filmmaking, music and journalism.

### 【你的答案】
1. race | 2. ______ | 3. ______ | 4. ______ | 5. ______ | 6. populated | 7. rated | 8. ______ | 9. capital | 10. ______

### 【正确答案】
1. **race** | 2. **continual** | 3. **imaginary** | 4. **familiarize** | 5. **alarm** | 6. **rated** | 7. **capital** | 8. **expertise** | 9. **populated** | 10. **figures**

### 【错误原因分析】
**词库内混淆**:rated / capital / populated 三个词都在同一词库中,互换了位置,说明对它们的固定搭配不够熟悉:
- **rated** → be rated as(被评为)
- **capital** → venture capital(风险投资)
- **populated** → sparsely populated(人口稀少的)

### 【涉及知识点】
固定搭配、词性转换、语境辨析

### 【解题思路】
1. 先通读全题,标记已知搭配(如 race、continual、imaginary 等)
2. 对不确定的题,先看空格前后的**固定搭配信号词**:
   - "venture ___" → 固定术语 → **capital**
   - "be ___ as" → 被动 → **rated**
   - "sparsely ___" → 副词修饰 → **populated**
3. 排除法:词库中每个词只用一次,确定一个划掉一个

### 💡 举一反三
1. **be rated as** → The city is rated as one of the safest in the world.
2. **venture capital** → The startup raised $5 million in venture capital.
3. **sparsely populated** → Tibet is a sparsely populated region.
