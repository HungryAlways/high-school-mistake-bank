## 📝 题目 #1（汉译英）

### 【题目原文】
这名有经验的便衣警察有着很好的职业素养，因为他能短时间内在人群中发现他所遇到的小偷。(spot, come across)

### 【参考译文】
The experienced plainclothes policeman has good professional qualities, because he can **spot** the thief he **comes across** in the crowd in a short time.

### 【涉及知识点】
- spot v. （突然）发现、认出
- come across 偶然遇到/碰到
- 定语从句 the thief (that) he comes across

## 🎯 举一反三（本套核心考点）

**1. 半固定表达/习语（重点背诵）：**
- open one's eyes to sth. 使大开眼界
- keep one's eyes and ears open 眼观六路耳听八方
- have ... in common 有共同点
- keep up with 紧跟
- fresh in one's mind 记忆犹新
- by no means 决不（+倒装）

**2. 倒装句（第7、10题）：**
- Only + 状语（介短/从句）置于句首 → 主句部分倒装
  - Only by working hard can you succeed.
- 否定词（by no means / never / not until 等）置于句首 → 主句倒装
  - By no means should you give up.

**3. appeal 一词多义（第5、8题）：**
- appeal to sb. to do sth. 呼吁某人做某事
- appeal to sb. 打动/吸引某人
- 练习：The charity ______ to the public ______ donations.（appeal / for）→ appealed ... for

**4. worth 用法（第2题）：**
- be worth doing / be worth + n.
- 练习：The museum is well worth ______ (visit). → visiting
