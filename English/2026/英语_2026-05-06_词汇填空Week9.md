## 📝 错题 #1

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：词汇填空（Week 9）

### 【题目原文】
**Directions:** Complete the following passage by using the words in the box. Each word can only be used once. Note that there is one word more than you need.

**选项：** A. current | B. forks | C. fool | D. provides | E. conception | F. creatively | G. response | H. literally | I. moral | J. ignorant | K. switching

**The Big Decisions**

Let's say you had the chance to become a vampire. With one magical bite you would gain immortality and superhuman strength. Your friends who have undergone the transformation tell you the experience is incredible, saying everything about their new existence **(11)___** them with fun, companionship and meaning.

Would you do it, even knowing that once changed you could never go back?

The difficulty of the choice is that you'd have to use your human self and preferences to try to guess whether you'd enjoy becoming a vampire. You would **(12)___** become a different self. How can you possibly know what it would feel like to be this different version of you or whether you would like it?

In her book Transformative Experience, L. A. Paul, a philosophy professor at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, says life is filled with decisions that are a bit like this. Life is filled with **(13)___** in the road in which you end up changing who you are and what you want.

People who have a child suddenly become different. Joining the military is another transformational experience. So are marrying, changing careers, immigrating, **(14)___** beliefs. In each of these cases the **(15)___** you is trying to make an important decision, without having the chance to know what it will feel like to be the future you.

Paul's point is that we're fundamentally **(16)___** about many of the biggest choices of our lives and that it's not possible to make purely rational decisions. "You shouldn't **(17)___** yourself," she writes. "You have no idea what you are getting into."

So how do you make transformational decisions? You have to ask the right questions, Paul argues. Ask, "Do I have a profound desire to discover what it would be like to be this new me, to experience this new mode of living?" As she puts it, "The best **(18)___** to this situation is to choose based on whether we want to discover who we'll become."

Personally, I think Paul's solution is incomplete. I'd say to really make these decisions well you need to step outside the modern **(19)___** of ourselves as cognitive creatures. After all, we're **(20)___** creatures. These days we think of a lot of decisions as if they were shopping choices. When we're shopping for something, we act as autonomous creatures who are looking for the product that will produce the most pleasure or utility. But choosing to have a child or selecting a spouse, faith or life course is not like that. It's probably safer to ask "What do I admire?" than "What do I want?"

### 【你的答案】
(11) H. literally | (12) F. creatively | (13) B. forks | (14) D. provides | (15) A. current | (16) C. fool | (17) J. ignorant | (18) G. response | (19) K. switching | (20) I. moral

### 【正确答案】
(11) **D. provides** | (12) **H. literally** | (13) **B. forks** | (14) **K. switching** | (15) **A. current** | (16) **J. ignorant** | (17) **C. fool** | (18) **G. response** | (19) **E. conception** | (20) **I. moral**

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **(11) 词性判断失误**：看到 `___ sb. with sth.` 没有识别出 `provide sb. with sth.` 固定搭配，误选了副词 literally。应优先识别固定搭配。
2. **(12) 语义理解偏差**：creatively（有创意地）修饰 become 语义不通，literally 表示"真正地"，强调转变的真实性。
3. **(14) 平行结构遗漏**：前面全是动名词并列（marrying, changing, immigrating），后面也必须是动名词 switching，而非动词 provides。
4. **(16) 和 (17) 词性混淆**：ignorant（形容词）和 fool（动词）互换了。be ignorant about（不了解）vs. fool yourself（自欺欺人），注意词性匹配。
5. **(19) 搭配不熟悉**：the modern conception of ourselves（现代人对自我的认知），conception 表示"观念、概念"。

### 【涉及知识点】
- 固定搭配：provide sb. with sth. / be ignorant about / fool oneself / forks in the road
- 平行结构：动名词并列（marrying, changing, immigrating, switching）
- 词性判断：形容词（ignorant, current, moral）vs. 动词（provides, fool, switching）vs. 副词（literally）
- 抽象概念搭配：modern conception of ourselves

### 【解题思路】
1. **先判断词性**：根据空格前后结构判断需要名词、动词、形容词还是副词
2. **寻找固定搭配**：如 `___ sb. with sth.` → provides；`be ___ about` → ignorant
3. **注意平行结构**：看到前面是 -ing 形式，后面也必须是 -ing
4. **语义验证**：代入后检查是否符合上下文逻辑
5. **排除法**：用掉一个选项划掉一个，最后多余一个

### 💡 举一反三

1. **provide 搭配**：The school provides students **with** free meals. / Free meals are provided **for** students.
2. **ignorant 搭配**：He was **ignorant of** the dangers involved. = He was **ignorant about** the dangers.
3. **fork in the road**：We stand at a **fork in the road** — we must choose between two different paths.
4. **fool oneself**：Don't **fool yourself** into thinking it'll be easy.
