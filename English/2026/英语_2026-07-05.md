## 📝 错题 #1

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:单词默写(中译英)

### 【题目原文】
3. adj. 努力工作的,勤勉的 ____

### 【你的答案】
hard

### 【正确答案】
hardworking

### 【错误原因分析】
只写了 hard(努力的/坚硬的),漏掉了 working 部分。hardworking 是合成形容词,必须完整拼写。

### 【涉及知识点】
合成词 hard + working;易与 hard(硬的) 混淆

### 【解题思路】
"努力工作的" = hard(努力) + working(工作) → hardworking,一个词,中间无空格无连字符。

## 📝 词组错题 #1  题2 · 和谐相处
- 【你的答案】be harmonious with
- 【正确答案】**live in harmony (with)**
- 【分析】"和谐相处"固定短语是 live in harmony (with sb),harmonious 是形容词,不能说 be harmonious with 表"相处"。
- 【知识点】in harmony with 与...协调一致

## 📝 词组错题 #2  题7 · 身体健康/不健康
- 【你的答案】healthy body
- 【正确答案】**be in good/poor health**
- 【分析】表"身体健康状况"用 be in good health(健康)/ be in poor health(不健康),health 是名词。
- 【知识点】in good/poor health;keep healthy

## 📝 词组错题 #3  题9 · 向某人求助
- 【你的答案】to ask sb for help
- 【正确答案】**ask sb for help**(去掉 to)
- 【分析】多写了 to。ask sb for help = 向某人求助,无需 to。
- 【知识点】ask sb for sth 向某人要某物

## 📝 词组错题 #4  题10 · 碰巧
- 【你的答案】It's happen to do sth.
- 【正确答案】**It (so) happens that... / sb. happen to do sth.**
- 【分析】结构混乱。两种表达:1 sb happen to do sth(某人碰巧做)2 It (so) happens that + 句子。不能说 It's happen to。
- 【知识点】happen to do / It happens that

## 📝 词组错题 #5  题14 · 长大成人
- 【你的答案】grow up into the grown
- 【正确答案】**grow up**
- 【分析】画蛇添足。grow up 本身即"长大成人",不需加 into the grown。
- 【知识点】grow up 长大;grow into 长成为

## 📝 词组错题 #6  题15 · 养成好/坏习惯
- 【你的答案】develop the good/bad habits of
- 【正确答案】**form/develop a good/bad habit**
- 【分析】冠词/单复数错。养成"一个"习惯用 a good/bad habit(单数、不定冠词),不用 the ... habits of。
- 【知识点】form/develop a habit;get into the habit of doing

## 📝 词组错题 #7  题16 · 热烈的讨论/激烈的辩论
- 【你的答案】warmed/fierce discussion
- 【正确答案】**a heated discussion/argument**
- 【分析】warmed 用错。"热烈/激烈的讨论"固定说 a heated discussion,heated 表"激烈的"。
- 【知识点】heated discussion/argument/debate

## 📝 词组错题 #8  题17 · 一...就...
- 【你的答案】as soon as
- 【正确答案】**hardly...when / no sooner...than**
- 【分析】本题考"倒装"表达。hardly...when 和 no sooner...than 都表"一...就...",且句首常倒装。as soon as 虽也表"一...就",但此处要求这两个高级句型。
- 【知识点】Hardly had I...when...;No sooner had I...than...

## 📝 词组错题 #9  题22 · 令某人头疼的问题
- 【你的答案】headache for a problem
- 【正确答案】**a headache for sb.**
- 【分析】a headache 本身即"令人头疼的问题/麻烦",后接 for sb,不需再加 a problem。
- 【知识点】be a headache for/to sb 令某人头疼

## 📝 词组错题 #10  题23 · 导游
- 【你的答案】guide tourists
- 【正确答案】**tour/tourist guide**
- 【分析】语序/词性错。"导游(人)"是 tour guide 或 tourist guide,名词短语;guide tourists 意为"引导游客"(动词短语)。
- 【知识点】tour guide 导游

## 📝 词组错题 #11  题26 · 有助于(某人)做某事
- 【你的答案】be helpful for sb
- 【正确答案】**help (sb.) (to) do sth.**
- 【分析】本题要 help sb (to) do 结构;be helpful 后一般接 to sb / in doing,不能写 be helpful for sb 来表"有助于做某事"。
- 【知识点】help sb (to) do sth;be helpful to sb

## 📝 词组错题 #12  题28 · 坚持立场
- 【你的答案】on one's ground

- 【分析】漏动词 hold。hold one's ground = 坚持立场/毫不退让。

## 📝 词组错题 #13  题30 · 改掉坏习惯

## 📝 词组错题 #14  题31 · 利大于弊/弊大于利

## 📝 词组错题 #15  题36 · 礼堂,报告厅

## 📝 词组14 #1  题3 · 把...寄予厚望

## 📝 词组14 #2  题4 · 激发想象力
- 【你的答案】immitate imagination
- 【正确答案】**inspire/stimulate one's imagination**
- 【分析】双重错误:1 imitate(模仿)用错词,应为 inspire/stimulate(激发);2 immitate 拼写也错(正确 imitate)。

## 📝 词组14 #3  题9 · 为纪念;对...表示敬意
- 【你的答案】be honor for
- 【正确答案】**in honour of**

# 英语 Test 17 阅读理解（Part C · 文章C）错题

> 来源：高考英语 Test 17 阅读理解 文章C（励志故事："猫有九条命/我的第三次人生"——残疾作者雨夜爆胎，被小女孩和盲人爷爷热心相助）。4题错3题（1、2、3）。【完整原文已附】

## 【完整原文】

**C**

They say a cat has nine lives, and I think that possible since I am now living my third life and I'm not even a cat. My father died when I was 15, and we had a hard struggle to make a living. And my mother, who was seriously ill in her last years, died while still in her 60s. My sister married soon after, and I followed her example within the year.

This was when I began to enjoy my first life. I was very happy, in excellent health. I had a good job in San Jose and a beautiful home up the peninsula in San Carlos. Life was a pleasant dream. Then the dream ended. I became *afflicted*(使苦恼) with a slowly progressive disease of the motor nerves, affecting first my right arm and leg, and then my other side. Thus began my second life...

In spite of my disease I still drove to and from work each day, with the aid of special equipment installed in my car. And I managed to keep my health and optimism, to a degree, because of 14 steps. Crazy? Not at all. Our home was an affair with 14 steps leading up from the garage to the kitchen door. Those steps were a standard measure of life. They were my yardstick, my challenge to continue living. I felt that if the day arrived when I was unable to lift one foot up one step and then drag the other painfully after it—repeating the process 14 times, I would be through—I could then admit defeat and lie down and die.

Then on a dark night in August, 1971, I began my third life. It was raining when I started home that night; strong winds and slashing rain beat down on the car as I drove slowly down one of the less-traveled roads. Suddenly the steering wheel *jerked*(猛然一动). In the same instant I heard the bang of a blowout. It was impossible for me to change that tire! Utterly impossible! I started the engine and thumped slowly along until I came to a dirt road, where I turned in and where I found lighted windows welcomed me to a house. I pulled into the driveway and blared the horn.

The door opened and a little girl stood there. When she knew what happened to me, she went into the house and a moment later came out, followed by a man who called a cheerful greeting. I sat there comfortable and dry, and felt a bit sorry for the man and the little girl working so hard in the storm.

About an hour later, the man's voice was heard, "This is a bad night for car trouble, but you're all set now." "Thanks!" I said. "How much do I owe you?" He shook his head, "Nothing. Cynthia told me you were a cripple. Glad to be of help. I know you'd do the same for me. There's no charge, friend." I held out a five-dollar bill, "No! I like to pay my way." He made no effort to take it and the little girl stepped closer to the window and said quietly, "Grandpa can't see it."

## 📝 阅读C #1  第1题 · "A cat has nine lives" here means ___
- 【题干】"猫有九条命"在文中意为？
- 【选项】A. a cat can live nine times longer than any other animal　B. a cat can die ninth　C. a lucky man can not die easily　D. the writer will live nine times
- 【你的答案】B. a cat can die ninth（猫会第九个死——不通）
- 【正确答案】**C. a lucky man can not die easily（幸运的人不易死去）**
- 【分析】开头作者说"猫有九条命，我不是猫却在过第三次人生"——是借"猫有九条命"比喻**自己命大、大难不死**。B"a cat can die ninth"本身不成句、更非文章寓意。理解习语要结合下文作者三次"重生"的经历。
- 【知识点】主旨/词义猜测题：习语含义须结合上下文比喻义，不能字面直译

## 📝 阅读C #2  第2题 · What do you think of the man who helped change the tire?
- 【题干】你认为帮忙换胎的老人是个怎样的人？
- 【选项】A. Warm-hearted but pitiable（热心但可怜）　B. Warm-hearted and happy（热心而快乐）　C. A blind old man that has nothing to do every day　D. A poor old man that is always ready to help others
- 【你的答案】A. Warm-hearted but pitiable（热心但可怜）
- 【正确答案】**B. Warm-hearted and happy（热心而快乐）**
- 【分析】老人虽是盲人，但他"called a cheerful greeting"（愉快地打招呼）、乐于助人、不图回报——文章基调是**赞美、温暖、积极**，而非"可怜/pitiable"。作者要表达的是盲人自强乐观、助人为乐，不该用"可怜"评价他。人物态度题要抓住文中情感基调（cheerful/glad）。
- 【知识点】人物评价/态度题：抓住描写人物的褒义细节（cheerful, glad to help），避免主观加"可怜"

## 📝 阅读C #3  第3题 · How will the story be ended?
- 【题干】故事将如何结尾？
- 【选项】A. The writer paid the little girl but the old man did not accept.　B. The writer drove away with tears running down his cheek.　C. The writer stayed there, without knowing what to do and how to do.　D. In the next few frozen seconds the writer felt the shame and astonishment he had never felt before.
- 【你的答案】A. The writer paid the little girl but the old man did not accept.
- 【正确答案】**D. In the next few frozen seconds the writer felt the shame and astonishment he had never felt before.（接下来几秒作者僵住了，感到从未有过的羞愧与震惊）**
- 【分析】结尾小女孩轻声说"Grandpa can't see it"（爷爷看不见），点破老人竟是**盲人**却在暴雨中帮"自认残疾"的作者换胎——这一反转让作者深受震撼、羞愧（自己总强调身体残疾自怨自艾，盲人老人却乐观助人）。故事戛然而止于这种情感冲击，D 最合逻辑与情感高潮。A 只是表面情节，未抓住结尾的震撼。
- 【知识点】推理/情节续写题：抓住结尾反转（盲人助人）带来的情感冲击，选最能承接主旨升华的选项

## 📌 Test 17 阅读理解C 易错点（记叙文主旨+人物态度）

## 📌 词组测验6 重点难点提示（com-/con- 前缀集中）
- **易混介词**：congratulate sb. **on**（4）、be concerned **with/about**（10/37）、be confused **about**（13）、comment **on**（14）、concentrate **on**（23）、complain **about**（31）、be content(ed) **with**（47）
- **表状态 vs 表动作**：keep in contact with（21 保持）↔ make contact with（27 联络）↔ be out of contact with（42 失去联系）
- **固定结构**：take...into consideration（15）、compare A to B（11 比作）、confuse A with B（43 混淆）、consider...as/to be（25）
- **状语性短语**：considering（8）、all things considered（38）、as far as I am concerned（28）、comparatively speaking（36）
- **同义多说法**：be composed of = be comprised of = consist of（17）；in conclusion = to conclude（19）；draw/reach/come to/arrive at a conclusion（40）

## 📌 单词测验1 高频易错点（形近/近义混淆）
- **构词错误**：disadvantage（1）写成 unadvantage——否定前缀 dis-（不是 un-）
- **形近词混淆**（最集中）：
  - addict（7 瘾君子）≠ attract（吸引力）
  - venture（13 冒险）——误当 status/地位
  - admission（15 准许进入/承认）≠ admiration（尊敬）
  - affordable（17 负担得起）≠ avoidable（可避免的）
  - advisable（20 明智的）≠ advised/建议——形容词是"明智"不是"建议"
  - acknowledge（27 承认）≠ acquire（获取）
  - advocate（28 提倡）≠ fund/资助
  - adaptable（30 能适应的）≠ adjust（调整，动词）
  - accountant（34 会计）≠ account/数据
  - adore（38 热爱崇拜）≠ adorable/可爱
  - absolutely（39 绝对地）≠ accomplish/完成
  - additional（42 附加的）≠ in addition/除此之外——词性区别：形容词 vs 连接副词
  - advance（46 前进/促进）≠ expect/预期
  - accompany（47 陪伴/伴奏）≠ cooperate/合作
  - adviser（50 顾问，名词）≠ advise/建议（动词）——不是"建议者"，而是"顾问/导师"
- **拓展记忆**：aboard（16 在船上，adv./prep.）≠ abroad（在国外）≠ board（董事会/上船）
- **建议**：这份错题多为"英译中时形近词张冠李戴"，复习时把 addict/attract、admission/admiration、affordable/avoidable、adaptable/adjust 等形近词**成对辨析**
