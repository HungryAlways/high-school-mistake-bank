## 📝 错题 #1

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：完形填空 — 定语从句

**知识点**：关系副词 where、定语从句省略关系词

### 【题目原文】
Does the word "imperfect" always convey negative feelings? Have you ever walked to the local grocery store's produce section, only to see a basket full of "imperfect produce"? There's nothing wrong with a tomato (26) ____ isn't perfectly rounded or peaches in different sizes; they still carry the same benefits and flavors as the very versions (27) ____ we're used to seeing in grocery stores.

A growing group of grocery chains are now fighting against the situation (28) ____ farmers tend to throw away imperfect fruits and vegetables, as many grocery chains won't accept them for fear that they are unsellable. One such business is Imperfect Produce, a start-up in the US that delivers fresh ugly produce to consumers, with (29) ____ service, you can get up to 20 pounds of fruit and veggies for around $20 a week. This is about a 30 percent discount compared to (30) ____ is currently sold in stores. So far, the company has been accepted as partnership by several big chain wholesale giants, such as Whole Foods Company, to sell the misshapen produce.

Despite the fact (31) ____ this movement might be a new trend here in the US, it's already gained serious popularity in Europe. In 2014, (32) ____ the European Union announced the Year Against Food Waste, a French grocery chain launched a very successful campaign called Inglorious Fruits and Vegetables.

Buying these unfortunate-looking foods should be appealing to consumers not only due to the affordability, but also for the support it gives to farmers and the direct impact (33) ____ it has on decreasing food waste and the environmental pollution. It is believed (34) ____ when the discarded fruits and vegetables decompose (分解), they release methane, a greenhouse gas that, when released into the atmosphere, is about 86 times as powerful as carbon dioxide over a 20-year period.

It remains unknown (35) ____ such a movement will make a difference in the long term. However, we should all bear in mind that waste is, in fact, the ugliest thing of all.

26. A. what  B. that  C. when  D. in which
27. A. where  B. which  C. that  D. as
28. A. that  B. where  C. which  D. for which
29. A. which  B. its  C. this  D. whose
30. A. something  B. which  C. what  D. that
31. A. that  B. which  C. where  D. how
32. A. how  B. which  C. on which  D. when
33. A. /  B. what  C. how  D. where
34. A. why  B. /  C. how  D. that
35. A. that  B. whether  C. why  D. if

### 【你的答案】
28. A | 33. C

### 【正确答案】
28. **B. where**
33. **A. /（不填）**

### 【错误原因分析】
**第 28 题**：误选 that。先行词是 "situation"，从句 "farmers tend to throw away imperfect fruits and vegetables" 主谓宾齐全，句子完整，不需要关系代词，应选关系副词 where（= in which），表示"在这种情况下"。

**第 33 题**：误选 how。"how" 不能引导定语从句。从句 "it has on decreasing food waste..." 结构完整（it = support，has an impact on...），不需要任何关系词，直接省略。

### 【解题思路】
**第 28 题**：
1. 定位先行词：the situation
2. 分析从句结构：farmers（主）tend to throw away（谓）imperfect fruits and vegetables（宾）→ 完整
3. 从句完整 + 先行词表"场景/情境" → 用关系副词 where
4. where = in which，表示"在这种情境下"

**第 33 题**：
1. 定位先行词：the direct impact
2. 分析从句结构：it（主）has（谓）→ 缺的是 impact 的介词补足语，但定语从句中当先行词在从句中已有介词搭配（have an impact on）时，关系词可省略
3. 从句结构完整 → 不填任何词
4. 注意：how 不能引导定语从句，排除 C

### 【举一反三】
1. **改写练习**：用 where 改写以下句子：The teacher explained the situation which the students were confused about. → The teacher explained the situation ____ the students were confused about.
2. **改写练习**：在空格处填或不填：We need to understand the process ____ it works.（提示：从句完整吗？）
