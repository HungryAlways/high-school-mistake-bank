## 📝 错题 #2

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:选词填空 / 高中英语词汇运用

### 【题目原文】
Directions: Fill in each blank with a proper word chosen from the box. Each word can be used only once. Note that there is one word more than you need.

A. appetite B. approached C. dedicated D. especially E. humbled F. insight G. interest H. motivated I. socially J. subsequent K. update

**Why You Should Befriend the People You Admire**

At its purest, admiration has a kind of magical quality. In his classic *An Introduction to Social Psychology*, the British psychologist William McDougall described how we experience true admiration. "We are (11) ______ by its presence, and, in the case of a person whom we intensely admire we become shy, like a child in the presence of an adult stranger," he wrote.

This is how I felt the first time I met one of my scholarly heroes, the political scientist and Harvard professor James Q. Wilson. He was truly honest and willing to go wherever the data led him, without fear or favor. Yet he was famously a gentle soul, always ready to consider the views of others and (12) ______ his own. For unknown reasons, Wilson attended my dissertation defense. Although I passed the defense, it went poorly. Nonetheless, Wilson (13) ______ me afterward and offered to stay in touch as I entered academia.

In the (14) ______ years, James and I shared ideas. He wrote the foreword to my first commercial book and served on the committee of the think tank I later led. After his death in 2012, I gave a eulogy at his funeral and (15) ______ a book to his memory.

To this day, I believe that my friendship with James made me a better scholar and a better person. And indeed, research has demonstrated that when people admire others, they can learn how to behave better, desire to achieve ambitious goals, and are (16) ______ to improve themselves. Admiration is particularly powerful when those we admire are true friends. This is a common (17) ______ in many philosophical traditions.

In contrast, seeking admiration for its own sake is dangerous. "The '(18) ______ for applause,'" the Dutch political philosopher Jan-Willem van der Rijt has written, "counts amongst the lowest of human character traits." Scholars have shown that this desire is associated with narcissism and self-promotion. Beyond being (19) ______ destructive, it often leads to unhappiness.

Mutual admiration is the mark of what the ancient Greek philosopher Aristotle would call a "perfect friendship." But collecting friends who admire you more than vice versa-(20) ______ for your looks, money or status-is good for neither pursuing happiness nor becoming a better person.

### 【你的答案】
11. E  12. K  13. H  14. B  15. C  16. J  17. F  18. A  19. D  20. G

### 【正确答案】
**11. E (humbled)  12. K (update)  13. B (approached)  14. J (subsequent)  15. C (dedicated)  16. H (motivated)  17. F (insight)  18. A (appetite)  19. I (socially)  20. D (especially)**

**多余选项**:G. interest

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **第13题(H→B)**:词义辨析。approached = 主动接近/接触,afterward(事后)+ offered to stay in touch = 主动联系我。motivated = 激励,搭配是 motivate sb. to do,此处无 to do 结构,语法不对。
2. **第14题(B→J)**:固定搭配。In the **subsequent** years = 在随后的几年里,subsequent years 是固定搭配。approached 是动词,不能修饰名词 years。
3. **第16题(J→H)**:语法结构。are **motivated** to do = 有动力做......,be motivated to do 是固定结构。subsequent 是形容词,不能放在 are 后面接 to do。
4. **第19题(D→I)**:副词修饰形容词。修饰 destructive 需用副词。socially destructive = 对社会具有破坏性的,批判自恋对社会的危害。especially(尤其)语义不对。
5. **第20题(G→D)**:逻辑补充。破折号后补充说明钦佩的原因,**especially**(尤其)强调列举中的重点项--"尤其是因为外表、金钱、地位"。interest 语义不通。

### 【解题思路】
11. **humbled**:被动语态 + 语境。"We are humbled by its presence" = 被钦佩之情弄得谦卑/不自在。后文"变得害羞,像个孩子"印证。
12. **update**:并列动词 + 学术语境。consider the views of others and update his own = 考虑他人观点并**更新**自己的观点,符合学者根据数据调整观点的语境。
13. **approached**:动词过去式 + 时间提示。Wilson approached me afterward = 威尔逊**主动接近**我。afterward 提示过去式。
14. **subsequent**:形容词修饰名词。In the subsequent years = 在**随后的**几年里,固定搭配。
15. **dedicated**:动词搭配。dedicated a book to his memory = **将一本书献给**他的纪念,dedicate sth. to... 固定搭配。
16. **motivated**:分词作表语。are motivated to improve themselves = **有动力**提升自己,be motivated to do 固定结构。
17. **insight**:名词辨析。a common insight in many philosophical traditions = 许多哲学传统中常见的**见解**。
18. **appetite**:名词搭配。the appetite for applause = 对掌声的**渴望**,appetite for... 比喻用法。
19. **socially**:副词修饰形容词。socially destructive = **对社会**具有破坏性的,修饰形容词 destructive。
20. **especially**:副词。破折号后补充说明原因,especially 强调列举中的重点项--"尤其是因为外表、金钱、地位"。

### 💡 选词填空技巧
1. **先判词性**:看到空格先判断需要什么词性(动词/名词/形容词/副词),缩小范围
2. **找固定搭配**:dedicate...to...、be motivated to do、appetite for、subsequent years
3. **看上下文逻辑**:学者语境用 update(更新观点)、学术纪念用 dedicate(献给)
4. **注意并列结构**:consider... and update... 并列用动词原形
