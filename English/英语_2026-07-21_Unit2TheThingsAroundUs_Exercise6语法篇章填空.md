# Unit 2 The Things Around Us Exercise 6 语法篇章填空 The 1940s House

## 【完整原文】

**The 1940s House**

IWM historian Terry Charman takes us on a tour of the 1940s house. The house is a reproduction of the home __1__ the Hymers family lived in during the making of the Channel 4 series *The 1940s House* (2001).

**The Living Room**

In the Living room we see how families spent their spare time listening to the wireless and gramophone records. The family also used this room to sew and knit, having to "Make Do and Mend", __2__ clothes rationing (定量供给) introduced in June 1941.

**The Kitchen and the Dining Room**

We find it really hard to avoid hunger in that period. Food and fuel rationing affected activities in the kitchen. Butter and sugar were the first foods __3__ (ration) in January 1940, followed by meat rationing from March 1940.

Terry Charman takes us through a typical rationed meal in the dining room. In this room we can also see how the family prepared for *air raids* (空袭). To tell the truth, the focus in the dining room is __4__ on food rationing.

**The Boys' Bedroom**

In the Boys' bedroom we discover the toys that the boys played with. Toys became __5__ (difficult) to obtain as the war continued and companies such as Meccano produced weapons instead. We also see books with a wartime theme that the boys read.

**The Queue for the Fish Shop**

Although fresh fish was never rationed, it was often __6__ short supply, and quite expensive. Fish queues were always long and even air raids could not disperse them. Supplies were limited __7__ the Royal Navy requisitioned (征用) many fishing fleets and the German Navy and Air Force restricted the activity of the remaining fleets in the North Sea. In this audio clip, Lily Phillips __8__ (explain) how her mother used the black market to supplement their rations.

**Morrison Shelters**

This Ministry of Information drawing shows the multi-purpose Morrison Shelter. It was available from 1941 onwards, __9__ (provide) a solution for families without a garden, __10__ could not build an Anderson Shelter. ARP Wardens kept a list of houses that had a Morrison Shelter, so rescuers knew where they should dig to free the family beneath the rubble. In this audio clip, Ron Frost describes his family's Morrison Shelter.

## 【逐题错误分析】

### 第 1 题
- **which → where**：先行词是 the home，从句 "the family lived ___ during..." 缺**地点状语**，用关系副词 **where**（= in which）。若用 which，则句末需补介词（in which the family lived），单用 which 不能作状语。

### 第 2 题
- **to which → due to**：此处表原因"由于服装定量供给（于 1941 年 6 月开始实行）"，用介词短语 **due to** + 名词。to which 引导定语从句，此处无合适先行词，句意也不通。

### 第 3 题
- **to ration → rationed**：Butter and sugar 与 ration（定量配给）是被动关系——"最早**被**实行配给的食物"，用过去分词 **rationed** 作后置定语（= that were rationed）。to ration 是不定式主动，语义不符。

### 第 4 题
- **also → all**："the focus in the dining room is ___ on food rationing"，用副词 **all** 强调"焦点**全都**集中在食物配给上"。also 意为"也"，此处无并列对象，语义不符。

### 第 6 题
- **as → in**：固定短语 **in short supply**（供应短缺/紧张）。it was often **in** short supply（鱼常常供应紧张）。as 不能与 short supply 构成此意。

### 第 7 题
- **to → because**：空格连接前后两个完整句子（Supplies were limited / the Royal Navy requisitioned...），表原因，用连词 **because**（因为皇家海军征用了许多渔船……）。to（不定式/介词）不能连接两个句子。

### 第 8 题
- **explained → explains**：介绍音频剪辑内容用**一般现在时**，主语 Lily Phillips 是第三人称单数 → **explains**。前文 describes 也是同样时态，可作参照。

### 第 10 题
- **which → who**：先行词是 families（人），指人用关系代词 **who**；which 只能指物。who could not build an Anderson Shelter（那些无法建造安德森防空洞的家庭）。

## 【知识点】

1. **关系副词 where vs 关系代词 which**：从句缺**地点状语**用 where（=in which，第1题）；缺主/宾语才用 which。
2. **due to + 名词**：表原因的介词短语（第2题）；辨析 due to（因为）与关系从句结构。
3. **过去分词作后置定语（被动）**：foods **rationed**（被配给的食物，第3题）——逻辑主语是动作承受者。
4. **副词 all 表强调**：is **all** on...（全都集中在……，第4题）。
5. **介词固定搭配 in short supply**（供应短缺，第6题）。
6. **原因状语从句连词 because**：连接两个完整句子表原因（第7题）；不定式 to 不能连接句子。
7. **一般现在时介绍内容**：describes / **explains**（音视频内容描述用现在时，主谓一致，第8题）。
8. **关系代词 who vs which**：先行词指人用 who（families → who，第10题），指物用 which。
9. **现在分词作伴随状语**：It was available..., **providing** a solution...（主动，主语 It 发出 provide 动作，第9题，✓）。
10. **more + 形容词**：became **more difficult** to obtain（越来越难获得，第5题，✓）。

## 【举一反三】

1. This is the village __ I spent my childhood.
   参考：**where**（= in which，从句缺地点状语，village 是地点）
2. The letters __ (write) by the soldiers during the war are kept in the museum.
   参考：**written**（信是被士兵写的，过去分词作后置定语）
3. He failed the exam __ he didn't work hard enough.
   参考：**because**（连接两句表原因）
4. Water was __ short supply after the earthquake.
   参考：**in**（in short supply 固定搭配）
