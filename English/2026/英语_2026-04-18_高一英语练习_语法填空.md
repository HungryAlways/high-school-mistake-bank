## 高一英语练习语法和词汇，Section A

### 【原文】
> Electric Bike Ban in New York Hurts Food Delivery Workers
>
> A ban on electric bicycles in New York City is hurting delivery workers who depend on them to earn a living. Many of the workers are immigrants.
>
> Electric bicycles, or"e-bikes", look like regular bicycles, but they have electric-powered motors to assist riders in moving the bike forward. Most e-bikes reach speeds of about 32 kilometers an hour, but some can go much (26) ______ (fast).
>
> (27) ______ it is legal to own e-bikes in New York City, it is not legal to operate them. Officials there consider the dangerous use of e-bikes on streets and sidewalks as the reason (28) ______ the ban. Last year, the city announced severe measures (29) ______ (mean) to hold e-bike riders and restaurants that employ the riders responsible.
>
> E-bike operators can now be fined $500 for breaking the ban. The police (30) ______ also seize the bikes.
>
> Many of New York's delivery workers are Chinese immigrants in their 50s and 60s. Their job requires them to work quickly and for long hours (31) ______ (earn) enough money to live on.
>
> Delivery worker Deqing Lian said it is important to perform quality work (32) ______ their job also depends on tips. He added that when delivery workers are too slow, some people refuse to pay for the food, which makes the workers'supervisors angry.
>

>
> Do Lee is with the Biking Public Project, (34) ______ provides assistance to bicycle-related workers in New York City. He says the city's ban on e-bikes is unfairly targeting low-paid workers who largely come from the city's Latino and Asian communities. He does not accept the argument (35) ______ e-bikes present a danger to citizens. However, many New Yorkers are quick to blame e-bike riders for not being safe.

## 错题解析

### 第 26 题：形容词比较级
- **题目**：`much (26) ______ (fast)`
- **解析**：`much` 修饰比较级 → `much faster`（快得多）。`much`、`even`、`still` 等词常修饰比较级

### 第 30 题：情态动词
- **题目**：`The police (30) ______ also seize the bikes`
- **解析**：表示"能够/可以" → 情态动词 `can`。`can also seize` = 也可以没收。`who` 是关系代词，不能放在主语 `The police` 后作谓语动词

### 第 33 题：动名词被动
- **题目**：`(33) ______ (catch) breaking the ban`
- **解析**：`being caught` 作主语，动名词的被动形式。`being caught breaking the ban` = 被抓到违反禁令。`catching` 是主动形式，而此处主语是"被抓到"，需用被动 `being caught`
