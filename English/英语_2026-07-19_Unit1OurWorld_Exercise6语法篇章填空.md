## 📝 错题 #1

### 【题目原文】

**Why Is the Chinese New Year Called "the Spring Festival"?**

"The Spring Festival" refers to the same festival more commonly known in English as "the Chinese New Year". It is the biggest festival in China.

In China, the Spring Festival is the common name for the Chinese Lunar New Year. __(1)__ it's in winter, the name "the Spring Festival" has its reasons.

The first reason is that the Spring Festival does in fact take place when the northern hemisphere starts its gradual warming, having already passed __(2)__ (cold) part of the winter. In traditional East Asian *lunisolar calendars*（阴阳历）, which is where the ancient Chinese calendar originated from, a year __(3)__ (divide) into 24 solar terms. The first solar term __(4)__ (know) as *Lichun*（立春）"Start of Spring", sees the sun enter its position in *the celestial longitude*（黄经）that initiates the gradual warming of the year. It's mid-way __(5)__ the *winter solstice*（冬至）and the *spring equinox*（春分）. On the Gregorian Calendar, "Start of Spring" begins between January 21st and February 20th, which is when the Spring Festival typically __(6)__ (take) place.

"Start of Spring" was in fact historically celebrated as __(7)__ festival of its own, also known as "Farmers' Day". It included traditional practices __(8)__ *sacrifices and ceremonial offerings*（献祭）to gods related to agriculture. This leads to the second point — the celebration for this festival in China began in ancient times, when traditional calendars were primarily used for agricultural purposes.

Another reason for this chosen name, and also __(9)__ the Spring Festival is relatively long, is that at that time, the newly formed government of the Republic of China wanted to include festivals of all Chinese ethnic spring celebrations into one inclusive holiday that was not labeled as "the Chinese New Year" __(10)__ (respect) the different new year celebrations of other ethnic groups at different dates.

### 【你的答案 vs 正确答案】

| 空 | 你的答案 | 正确答案 | 对错 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Because/Besides | **Although** | ❌ |
| 2 | the coldest | **the coldest** | ✅ |
| 3 | divided | **is divided** | ❌ |
| 4 | known | **known** | ✅ |
| 5 | between | **between** | ✅ |
| 6 | taken | **takes** | ❌ |
| 7 | that/which | **a** | ❌ |
| 8 | which/that | **such as** | ❌ |
| 9 | because | **why** | ❌ |
| 10 | respecting | **to respect** | ❌ |

### 【错误原因分析】

- **空1 → Although**：前后为让步关系（虽然在冬天，但叫春节自有原因），用 Although/Though。Because/Besides 不符合逻辑。
- **空3 → is divided**：主语 a year 与 divide 是被动关系（年被划分为24节气），一般现在时陈述事实 → is divided。只写 divided 漏了 be 动词，被动不完整。
- **空6 → takes**：主语 the Spring Festival 第三人称单数，一般现在时 → takes；且 take place 是主动短语，不能用被动。
- **空7 → a**：festival 可数名词单数，泛指"一个节日" → 不定冠词 a。此处非定语从句，不填 that/which。
- **空8 → such as**：后接举例（列举传统习俗）→ 固定搭配 such as（例如）。
- **空9 → why**：句型 The reason why ... is that ...，why 引导定语从句修饰 reason，不用 because。
- **空10 → to respect**：表目的（设立统一节日是为了尊重各民族新年）→ 不定式 to respect 作目的状语。respecting 不表目的。

### 【涉及知识点】
1. 让步状语从句连词 although/though
2. 一般现在时被动语态 is done
3. take place 主动短语 + 主谓一致
4. 冠词 a/an 泛指用法
5. such as 举例固定搭配
6. The reason why... is that... 句型（why 引导定语从句）
7. 不定式 to do 作目的状语

### 【举一反三】

1. （用 The reason why... is that... 句型）
   The reason ____ she was late is that her car broke down.
   （答案：why）

2. （such as / for example 辨析，选词填空）
   Many countries, ____ Japan and Korea, celebrate the Lunar New Year.
   （答案：such as）

3. （被动语态）
   The cake ____ (cut) into eight pieces before the party.
   （答案：is cut / was cut，依语境）
