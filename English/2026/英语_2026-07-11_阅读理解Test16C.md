## 【完整原文】

**Dear Alcohol,**

You've been around forever. I can remember all the pain you've caused me.

Do you remember the night you almost took my father's life? He loves you. Sometimes I think he loves you more than he loves me. He's addicted to you, to the way you promise to rid him of his problems only to provoke more of them. You just sat back and laughed as his car went spinning through the street, crashing into two cars. He wasn't the only one hurt by you that night.

Do you remember the night of my first high school party? You were there. My friends were intrigued by you. They treated you as if they were never going to see you again, consuming all of you that they could. I spent two hours that night helping my friends who had fallen head over heels for you. "I'm so embarrassed," they said as I held their hair back so that they could vomit. "I'm sorry," they said when I called cabs for them, walking them out and paying the driver in advance. "This won't happen again," they said as they were carted to the hospital to have their stomachs pumped. Two 15-year-old girls slept in hospital beds that night thanks to you.

Do you remember the night when you took advantage of my 17-year-old neighbor who had to drive to pick up his sister from her dance lessons? Do you know how we all felt when he hit another car head-on and killed the two people in the other car? He died the next morning too. His sister walked home from her dance lesson, and passed police cars and a crowd of people gathering on the sidewalk just two blocks away from the dance studio. She didn't realize her brother was in the midst of it all. She never saw him again. And it's all your fault.

I wish you'd walk out of my life forever. I don't want anything to do with you. Look at all the pain you've caused. Sure, you've made people happy too from time to time. But the damage you've caused in the lives of millions is inexcusable. Stop tricking the people I love. Stop hurting me, please.

Sincerely,
Me

## 【逐题错误分析】

### 第2题 ❌ D were fed up with → ✅ B were curious about
- 原句：My friends **were intrigued by** you. They treated you as if they were never going to see you again, consuming all of you that they could.
- 分析：intrigue = **激起……的好奇/兴趣**，were intrigued by = **对……感到好奇/被吸引**。下文"拼命喝酒、当作再也见不到"说明朋友们对酒**充满好奇/兴趣**，不是"厌倦(fed up with)"。
- 你的失误：把 intrigued 望文生义或凭感觉选了反义的 fed up with（厌烦）。**词义题一定要看上下文的行为**：拼命喝 = 好奇/着迷，不可能是厌倦。

### 第3题 ❌ D → ✅ C He crashed into a car from the other direction
- 原句：he **hit another car head-on** and killed the two people in the other car
- 分析：**head-on = 迎面地、正面地**，hit another car head-on = **迎面撞上对向来的车**。C"撞上来自对向的车"正是这个意思。
- 排除其他项：
  - A：他去接的是妹妹（放学/舞蹈课），妹妹没喝酒，错
  - B：不是妹妹的错，错
  - D：妹妹是"**没意识到(didn't realize)**哥哥在事故中"，不是"吓得不敢看"，错
- 你的失误：D 的干扰在于"她走过事故现场"，但原文明说她**不知情(didn't realize)**，不是"吓得不敢看"。要抠原文细节，不能脑补。

## 📌 阅读理解解题技巧小结

**① 词义猜测题（第2题类）**
- 别望文生义，**看上下文的具体行为/例子**
- intrigued by = 好奇/被吸引（下文"拼命喝"印证）
- 常见近义：be curious about / be fascinated by / be attracted to

**② 细节理解题（第3题类）**
- 抠原文关键词：head-on = 迎面/正面相撞（对向来车）
- 干扰项常"偷换细节"：把"没意识到(didn't realize)"偷换成"吓得不敢看(too scared to look)"
- **回原文定位，逐项对照，不能凭印象脑补**

**③ 基调/态度题（第4题）**
- 全文控诉酒精害人（父亲、朋友、邻居惨死）→ critical（批判的）
- 看作者的情感词：pain、fault、inexcusable、Stop hurting → 明显批判

**④ 重点短语积累**
- be intrigued by 对……好奇
- head-on 迎面地/正面地
- take advantage of 趁机利用/占便宜
- fall head over heels for 深深迷恋
- rid sb. of 使某人摆脱

## 🎯 举一反三（自测练习）

3. 辨析：be fed up with / be curious about / be familiar with 各是什么意思？
4. 短语造句：用 take advantage of 造一个句子。
5. 态度题技巧：判断作者态度，应重点关注文中哪类词？
