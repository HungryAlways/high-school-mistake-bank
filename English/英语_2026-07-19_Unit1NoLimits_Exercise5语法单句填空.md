# Unit 1 No Limits Exercise 5 语法单句填空（非谓语动词作定语）

## 【完整原文】

1. We must find ways __1__ (coexist) and thrive with our local animals and plants.
2. I feel so bored as there is nothing __2__ (do) right now. How about playing chess with me?
3. Babies often weep to have adults know their urge __3__ (comfort) when they are alone, afraid of silence and darkness.
4. Sir, could you please give me more paper __4__ (write) on? I have to say this math test is really difficult.
5. The robot was created by Isabel Myers, intended for all who had a strong desire __5__ (free) from domestic chores.
6. As one of the key researchers in this chemistry lab, Johnson has a lot of experiments __6__ (conduct) every day.
7. It is our duty __7__ (foster) a more loving and harmonious social environment where all humanity is treated fairly and equally.
8. Some students fail to resist the temptation __8__ (admit) to "the shopping club of brand goods" due to the appealing pictures and words in advertisements.
9. We should adopt personality tests as a tool __9__ (develop) ourselves rather than view it as a limit on ourselves or an easy way to define others.
10. A 25-year-old boxer from the southern Indian state of Telangana has become the fifth Indian woman __10__ (win) a gold medal at a world championship.
11. ISPO will be the last opportunity for companies to participate in the largest European outdoor sector market research ever __11__ (undertake) this year to look at the size of the market.
12. Cancelling the written test is to some extent unfair because the test could have afforded students a fair opportunity to demonstrate their ability __12__ (express) themselves.
13. It would not be an exaggeration __13__ (say) that the vessel couldn't have been used by ancient people at that time, given its distinctive shape and fine craftsmanship.
14. Everyone, including those people who have suffered from the virus before, has the tendency __14__ (infect) with the mutated coronavirus if not wearing masks in high-risk areas.
15. At 12 years old, she won a Guinness Record when she became the youngest female __15__ (win) a world title for platform diving at the World Championships.
16. Right of first refusal (ROFR) is a contractual right __16__ (enter) a business transaction with a person or company before anyone else can.
17. With so many paths __17__ (choose) from and so many doubts about the future, where to find a job is a question that 10.76 million college graduates (a record number) are now being faced with.
18. Briefer and his team have designed an app that can tell whether an individual pig is experiencing a positive emotion, which is in line with the current intention __18__ (improve) the welfare of their animals.
19. Rachel Carson's classic best-seller about ecological threats, *Silent Spring*, played a direct role in the 1972 decision by the newly-formed U.S. Environmental Protection Agency __19__ (ban) the use of DDT, a poisonous pesticide.
20. Students learn new concepts more fully and retain the knowledge for longer periods of time when encouraged to make an attempt __20__ (involve) in finding a solution to a problem before getting instruction on exactly how to do it.

## 【逐题错误分析】

### 第 3 题
- **to comfort → to be comforted**：their urge（婴儿的渴望）与 comfort 是被动关系——婴儿是"被安慰"的对象，不是去安慰别人。名词后作定语的不定式，若逻辑主语是动作承受者，用 to be done。
- 判断法：把空还原成句子 "their urge is ___ " → the baby is comforted（婴儿被安慰）→ 被动。

### 第 5 题（按建议处理）
- **to free → to be free**：free 作动词是"解放/使自由"（及物），desire to free 会缺宾语；此处 free 用作形容词 "free from domestic chores"（摆脱家务），故用 to be free from。
- desire (to be free from ...) = 渴望摆脱……

### 第 7 题
- **fostering → to foster**：这是 "It is one's duty to do sth."（做某事是某人的职责）的固定句型，it 是形式主语，真正主语是不定式 to foster，不能用动名词 fostering。

### 第 8 题
- **to admit → to be admitted**：the temptation（诱惑）与 admit 是被动关系——学生是"被接纳"进俱乐部，admit sb. to（接纳某人加入）。逻辑主语 students 是 admit 的承受者，用 to be admitted。
- 对比：the temptation to do（主动去做）vs the temptation to be done（被……）。此处是"被接纳的诱惑"。

### 第 11 题
- **to undertake → to be undertaken**：market research（市场调研）与 undertake（开展）是被动关系——调研是被开展的。research (that is) to be undertaken this year = 今年将被开展的调研。

### 第 14 题
- **to infect → to be infected**：the tendency（趋势/倾向）修饰的逻辑主语是 everyone，人是"被感染"的对象——be infected with（被……感染）。故 the tendency to be infected（有被感染的趋势）。
- infect 是及物动词"感染（他人）"；"人被病毒感染"必须用被动。

### 第 15 题
- **winning → to win**：序数词/形容词最高级（the youngest female）后作定语，惯用不定式 to do，不用现在分词。the fifth/youngest ... to do sth. 是固定搭配（同第10题 the fifth Indian woman to win）。

### 第 17 题
- **being chosen → to choose**：这里是 "so many paths to choose from"（这么多路可供选择）。choose from 是主动搭配（从中选择），逻辑上人去 choose，用主动 to choose。paths to choose from = 可供选择的道路。
- 注意 from 不能丢，choose from 是固定搭配。

### 第 18 题
- **to be improved → to improve**：the intention（意图）与 improve 是主动关系——他们打算去改善（intend to improve）。intention to do sth.（做某事的意图），主动。
- 对比第3/8/11/14/20题：那些空的逻辑主语是动作承受者（被动）；本题逻辑主语（they）是动作发出者（主动）。

### 第 20 题
- **to involve → to be involved**：make an attempt（尝试）的逻辑主语是 students，学生是"被卷入/参与"解决问题——be involved in（参与、投入）。故 an attempt to be involved in。
- involve sb. in（使某人参与）是及物用法；"学生参与其中"用被动 be involved in。

## 【知识点】

**核心考点：名词/代词后不定式作定语时，主动 to do 还是被动 to be done？**

1. **判断口诀**：看空前的名词与括号动词的逻辑关系——
   - 名词是动作的**发出者**（主动）→ **to do**（如第18题 intention to improve）
   - 名词是动作的**承受者**（被动）→ **to be done**（如第3、8、11、14、20题）
   - 还原法：把"名词 + 不定式"还原成句子，看名词做主语时是主动还是被动。

2. **本卷被动题（to be done）**：urge to be comforted / temptation to be admitted / research to be undertaken / tendency to be infected / attempt to be involved。

3. **固定句型/搭配**：
   - It is one's duty **to do** sth.（第7题，形式主语）
   - the +序数词/最高级 + n. **to do**（第10、15题：the fifth woman to win / the youngest female to win）
   - n. **to do from**：paths to choose from（第17题，主动+介词）
   - desire/ability/intention/opportunity/right **to do**（第5、12、16、18题）

4. **易混**：animal/人"被感染 be infected"、"被接纳 be admitted"、"被开展 be undertaken" 都是及物动词，逻辑主语是人/物承受动作时必须被动。

## 【举一反三】

1. There is nothing __ (worry) about; everything is under control.
   参考：**to worry**（nothing to worry about，主动+介词，固定搭配）
2. The old house __ (repair) has been left unattended for years.
   参考：**to be repaired**（房子是被修的，被动定语）
3. He was the first student __ (hand) in the exam paper in our class.
   参考：**to hand**（the first ... to do，序数词后主动不定式）
