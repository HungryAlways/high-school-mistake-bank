## 【完整原文】（空格留空，可当练习重做）

Whenever I travel, I meet international leaders who have studied in Britain. These energetic, intelligent people chose Britain because we offer high quality higher education. This is good news for the UK. People who are educated here have a __(1)__ tie to our country. They promote Britain around the world, helping our trade and our diplomacy. It is easier for our executives and our diplomats to do business with people __(2)__ with Britain. British exports of education and training are worth some eight billion pounds a year. Money feeds into our institutions and helps our __(3)__ to open up opportunities for more people to study. Our young people also __(4)__. They gain from the contact with international students. We can teach, but we can also learn from others.

　　Nowadays, we are launching a long-term strategy to strengthen the United Kingdom as a first choice for the quality of study and the quality of our __(5)__ to international students. This will be a UK-wide and a Government-wide effort. We are __(6)__ a package of measures to help encourage students from overseas to study in the UK. We will offer international students more open doors...

　　We have the measures in place, but we are also __(7)__ tough targets for *recruitment* (招募). To business—I ask you to __(9)__ with the British Council overseas to market education. And to universities and colleges—I ask you to live up to your __(10)__, to professionalise your approach, to deliver a quality education to overseas students that encourages involvement and rises to the challenge of our competitors, to work as partners together.

## 【逐空错误分析】

### 第1空 ❌ J introducing → ✅ G lasting
- 原句：People who are educated here have a __(1)__ tie to our country.
- 分析：空格修饰名词 tie（联系），要用**形容词**。a **lasting** tie = 持久的联系（在英国受教育的人与英国有长久的纽带）。introducing 是现在分词/动名词，不能这样修饰 tie。**先看词性：修饰名词的空 → 形容词**。

### 第4空 ❌ B welcome → ✅ A benefit
- 原句：Our young people also __(4)__. They gain from the contact with international students.
- 分析：句子缺**谓语动词**，主语 young people。benefit（受益）作动词。下一句 "They gain from..."（他们从中获益）是同义复现，印证是 benefit。welcome 放这里句子不成立（缺谓语的动词义）。

### 第5空 ❌ H reputation → ✅ B welcome
- 原句：the quality of study and the quality of our __(5)__ to international students
- 分析：`the quality of our ___ to sb.` 要填名词，welcome（n. 欢迎/接待）：对国际学生的**欢迎/接待**质量。与前面 "quality of study" 并列。reputation to sb. 搭配不通。

### 第6空 ❌ G lasting → ✅ J introducing
- 原句：We are __(6)__ a package of measures to help encourage students...
- 分析：`We are ___ + 宾语` 是现在进行时，需要**现在分词**作谓语。introducing a package of measures = 推出一揽子措施。lasting 是形容词，不能作进行时谓语。

### 第10空 ❌ I goal → ✅ H reputation
- 原句：I ask you to live up to your __(10)__
- 分析：**live up to one's reputation** = 不负声誉/名副其实（这是固定搭配！要大学"不负声誉"）。goal 是本题**多余词**，你误用在此，导致 benefit 反而没地方用。

## 📌 选词填空技巧小结（针对你的"连环串位"）

**⚠️ 你最大的问题：一处填错，引发连锁错位**
- 你把 benefit→welcome→reputation→goal 整体后移一位（#4填welcome、#5填reputation、#10填goal），结果 benefit 空着没用
- **补救方法**：做完后一定要**核对"多余词"**——本题多余词应是 goal，如果你发现自己把 goal 用掉了、却剩下 benefit，说明中间串位了，要回头检查

**① 先定词性（避免串位的根本方法）**
- 修饰名词的空 → 形容词：#1 lasting（a lasting tie）
- `We are ___ + 宾语` → 现在分词：#6 introducing
- 主语后缺谓语 → 动词：#4 benefit
- `the quality of our ___` → 名词：#5 welcome

**② 认准固定搭配（送分）**
- a lasting tie 持久的纽带
- be familiar with 熟悉
- live up to one's **reputation** 不负声誉（#10 关键搭配）
- work with sb. 与……合作
- benefit from / gain from 从……受益

**③ 同义复现找线索**
- #4 benefit ← 下句 "They **gain** from..."（gain = benefit 同义）
- 篇章题里，空格答案常在**前后句有同义词印证**
