## 📌 高频易错搭配小结

**① 介词固定搭配（重点）**
| 词组 | 正确介词 |
|---|---|
| a barrier **to** | 对…的障碍 |
| attitude **to/towards** | 对…的态度 |
| pay attention **to** / attach importance **to** | to 是介词 |
| be based **on/upon** | 以…为基础 |
| be associated **with** | 与…有关 |
| ban sb. **from** doing | 禁止 |

**② 写作高级句型**
- It is generally believed/thought that...（人们普遍认为）
- It was (not) some time before...（没过多久就…）
- keep/bear in mind that...（牢记）

**③ 易混动词短语**
- attend（出席）↔ attend to（照料/处理）
- believe（相信话）↔ believe in（信任/信仰）
- attention to / importance to（to 后接名词或动名词）

**④ 语法特点词**
- belong to / come into being：**无被动、无进行时**
- avoid **doing**（只接动名词）
- attend school/church：**零冠词**
