## 阅读理解错题整理（第 62-66 题）

### 【完整原文】
> The search for new, clean energy sources has occupied the attention of scientists and politicians for years. One common (46) ______ for green energy is the wind. A new twist on this old resource could cause the energy output of wind-power plants to increase greatly.
>
> Standard wind-power plants rely on strong support and (47) ______ can only reach a height of 200 meters or so. Higher than that, winds tend to be stronger and steadier, but the challenge is (48) ______ how to obtain the energy from those winds.
>
> Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands and Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences in Germany have formed a kite-power research group. The group is working to develop kites with inflatable (可充气的) wings (49) ______ to electrical generators (发电机) on the ground.
>
> The research group's goal is to design a kite that can operate on its own for 24 hours. Research has begun, but many challenges remain, including making the generators more (50) ______ and perfecting the automatic flight control and the structure of the kites. Nevertheless in June 2012, they demonstrated that their kites could operate (51) ______ at an altitude of up to 700 meters.
>
> Meanwhile, in Italy, researchers are working on a(n) (52) ______ power generator that relies on kites when the generator called KiteGen, senses the wind blowing, kites are (53) ______ from the ends of poles with high-resistance cables to control their height and angle. These cables are able to move the kites if the system senses coming objects (54) ______ planes, helicopters or even individual birds. The kites themselves are light, tough and able to reach fairly high altitudes. They loop (绕行) around in the wind, which sets the center of the generator in motion, (55) ______ electric current.
>
> KiteGen has the (56) ______ to be very cost-effective in the long run. After the original cost of designing and setting up the plant, little (57) ______ investment will be necessary, apart from standard maintenance. The plant also requires (58) ______ little space, which makes it ideal for cities and means that multiple plants can be set up to provide even more (59) ______.
>
> Kite power has the potential to greatly (60) ______ on current wind-power strategies. In the future, it may be an efficient, cost-effective supplement to the other sources of energy we use, or even a replacement for some of them.

---

### 第 62 题（第 49 题）：connected to（连接到）
- **题目**：`The group is working to develop kites with inflatable wings ______ to electrical generators on the ground.`
- 选项：A. chosen | B. applied | C. collected | D. connected
- ❌ 错误答案：**B (applied)**
- ✅ 正确答案：**D (connected)**
- **解析**：`connected to` = **连接到**。风筝的充气翼需要**连接到**地面的发电机。`applied to` = 应用于，语义不符——翼不是"应用于"发电机，而是物理连接。
- **💡 记忆**：`connect A to B` = 把 A 连接到 B（物理连接）

### 第 63 题（第 50 题）：efficient（高效的）
- **题目**：`...including making the generators more ______ and perfecting the automatic flight control...`
- 选项：A. efficient | B. complete | C. powerful | D. positive
- ❌ 错误答案：**C (powerful)**
- ✅ 正确答案：**A (efficient)**
- **解析**：`efficient` = **高效的**。`making the generators more efficient` = 让发电机更高效。虽然 `powerful`（强大的）语义上看似合理，但结合上下文，研究目标是让风筝**独立运行 24 小时**，强调的是**效率**而非单纯的力量。
- **💡 解题**：结合上下文——"独立运行 24 小时"需要的是效率，而非单纯的力量

### 第 64 题（第 54 题）：such as（例如）
- **题目**：`...if the system senses coming objects ______ planes, helicopters or even individual birds.`
- 选项：A. except for | B. instead of | C. such as | D. due to
- ❌ 错误答案：**B (instead of)**
- ✅ 正确答案：**C (such as)**
- **解析**：`such as` = **例如**，用于列举。`objects such as planes, helicopters or even birds` = **例如飞机、直升机甚至鸟类等物体**。`instead of`（而不是）语义完全相反——系统不是"代替"飞机，而是"包括"飞机等物体。
- **💡 记忆**：`such as` = 举例（后面接具体例子）

### 第 65 题（第 58 题）：relatively（相对地）
- **题目**：`The plant also requires ______ little space...`
- 选项：A. concretely | B. abstractly | C. thoroughly | D. relatively
- ❌ 错误答案：**A (concretely)**
- ✅ 正确答案：**D (relatively)**
- **解析**：`relatively little space` = **相对较少**的空间。`relatively` = 相对地，修饰 `little`。`concretely`（具体地）语义不通——"具体地需要很少空间"不合逻辑。
- **💡 记忆**：`relatively + 形容词` = 相对…的（如 `relatively small` 相对较小）

### 第 66 题（第 60 题）：improve on（改进/优于）
- **题目**：`Kite power has the potential to greatly ______ on current wind-power strategies.`
- 选项：A. focus | B. extend | C. improve | D. transfer
- ❌ 错误答案：**D (transfer)**
- ✅ 正确答案：**C (improve)**
- **解析**：`improve on` = **改进、优于**。`improve on current wind-power strategies` = **改进现有的风力发电策略**。`transfer on` 搭配不当，`transfer` 通常接 `transfer to`（转移到）。
- **💡 记忆**：`improve on` = 在…基础上改进（比现有的更好）
