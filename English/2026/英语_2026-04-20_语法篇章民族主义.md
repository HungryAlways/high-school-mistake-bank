## 📝 错题 #5

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:综合英语 · 语法篇章填空
**知识点**:固定搭配、非谓语动词、反身代词、从句引导词、定语从句

### 【题目原文】

**The Rising Trend of Nationalism and Anti-Globalization**

Politically, countries are moving towards a free market and competing **(1)** ______ additional foreign investments. Recently, however, these trends seem **(2)** ______ (go) in a slightly different direction.

Brexit, for example, had been in the media spotlight for the past several years. The word is shorthand for Britain's separation from the European Union. First **(3)** ______ (bring) to the surface by a referendum in 2013, it had always been a controversial topic. Supporters of Brexit wanted sovereignty, the power that a country has to govern **(4)** ______ both economically and politically. They were eager to leave the EU because they felt **(5)** ______ the organization had been off the track and would no longer provide them with enough benefits as a member. **(6)** ______ the sentiment of nationalism increased, so did people's willingness to pull back after years of social mobility and cultural exchanges. In the end, Britain officially left the EU on Jan. 21, 2020. Undoubtedly, Brexit, **(7)** ______ outcome will be unpredictable in the next few years, will be sure to transform the lives of many, and however, whether the U.K. will change for the better in the long run remains **(8)** ______ (see).

The Italian government is also going through some intense criticism from its people. There has been a constant voice against immigration and the European Union in Italy. Some people are in favor of Italy **(9)** ______ (pull) back from the EU and deporting 500,000 illegal immigrants that were already in Italy.

Britain and Italy are just two examples of the surfacing trend of nationalism. Many other countries are tightening up on trade negotiations and foreign policies as well. Who knows **(10)** ______ will happen in the next five years? The effects of these changes are unknown and can only be discovered in the future.

### 【你的答案】

> 1.from · 2.to have gone · 3.to be brought · 4.it · 5.in which · 6.As · 7.who · 8.seen · 9.pulling · 10.what

### 【正确答案】

> 1.**for** · 2.**to have been going** · 3.**brought** · 4.**itself** · 5.**as if** · 6.**As** · 7.**whose** · 8.**to be seen** · 9.**pulling** · 10.**what**

### 【答题统计】

**4/10 正确** -- 错了 6 道(第 1、2、3、4、5、7、8 题)

### 【错题逐题分析】

#### ❌ 第 1 题:from → for
> "countries are moving towards a free market and competing **(1)** ______ additional foreign investments"

**为什么选 for:**
- **compete for sth.** = 争取/竞争某物,固定搭配
- 句意:各国正走向自由市场,**争取**更多外国投资

❌ **你的答案问题**:compete 不与 from 搭配。compete 的常见搭配:
- **compete for** + 目标(竞争某物)
- **compete with/against** + 对手(与谁竞争)

---

#### ❌ 第 2 题:to have gone → to have been going
> "these trends seem **(2)** ______ (go) in a slightly different direction"

**为什么选 to have been going:**
- **seem to have been doing** = 似乎一直在做(不定式的**完成进行时**)
- 强调趋势**从过去某个时间开始,一直持续到现在**的方向变化过程
- 时间状语 "in the past few years" 暗示**持续性**

❌ **你的答案问题**:to have gone 是不定式完成时,只表示"已经完成",**缺少持续性**。趋势的变化是渐进的、持续的过程,不是瞬间完成的动作。

💡 **对比**:
- seem to have gone = 似乎已经走了(已完成,强调结果)
- seem to have been going = 似乎一直在朝......方向走(进行中,强调过程)

---

#### ❌ 第 3 题:to be brought → brought
> "First **(3)** ______ (bring) to the surface by a referendum in 2013"

**为什么选 brought:**
- 这是**过去分词作状语**的省略结构
- 完整形式:First **(when it was)** brought to the surface...
- **bring to the surface** = 使浮出水面、暴露出来
- Brexit 与 bring 是**被动关系**(Brexit 被公投暴露出来),直接用过去分词 brought

❌ **你的答案问题**:to be brought 是不定式被动式,但这里不是目的/将来,而是**已经发生**的事情(2013年),用过去分词表已完成的被动即可。

💡 **规律**:when/while/before/after 等引导的时间/条件状语从句,如果主语与主句一致且含 be 动词,可以省略主语 + be,只保留分词。

---

#### ❌ 第 4 题:it → itself
> "the power that a country has to govern **(4)** ______ both economically and politically"

**为什么选 itself:**
- **govern itself** = 自治、自主治理
- **sovereignty(主权)** 的定义就是国家**自主治理**的能力
- 主语 country 和宾语是**同一个对象**,必须用反身代词

❌ **你的答案问题**:it 是普通代词,不能表示"反身"。govern it 意味着"治理它(别的东西)",语义不对。

---

#### ❌ 第 5 题:in which → as if
> "they felt **(5)** ______ the organization had been off the track"

**为什么选 as if:**
- **as if** = 好像、似乎,引导**表语从句/宾语从句**
- 后面用**虚拟语气**(had been),表示"感觉好像......(其实未必)"
- 句意:他们觉得**好像**这个组织已经偏离了正轨

❌ **你的答案问题**:in which 是关系代词结构,需要前面有先行词。felt 后面没有先行词,in which 无从指代。

💡 **识别技巧**:看到后面是一个完整句子(the organization had been...),且表达"好像"的语义,优先考虑 as if / as though。

---

#### ❌ 第 7 题:who → whose
> "Brexit, **(7)** ______ outcome will be unpredictable in the next few years"

**为什么选 whose:**
- **whose** 是关系代词,表示**所属关系** = "......的"
- **whose outcome** = Brexit 的结果
- 句意:Brexit 的结果在未来几年难以预测

❌ **你的答案问题**:who 只能指**人**,不能指物(Brexit)。whose 既可指人也可指物,表示"......的"。

💡 **识别技巧**:空后面是**名词**(outcome),说明需要所有格 → 选 whose。

---

#### ❌ 第 8 题:seen → to be seen
> "whether the U.K. will change for the better in the long run remains **(8)** ______ (see)"

**为什么选 to be seen:**
- **remain to be done** = 有待被......、尚未可知
- **remains to be seen** = 还有待观察/尚未可知,**固定表达**
- 句意:英国长期来看是否会变得更好,**还有待观察**

❌ **你的答案问题**:seen 单独使用不能构成这个固定表达。remain 后面接过去分词表示状态(如 remain unknown),但"有待做"必须用不定式。

💡 **对比**:
- remain done = 保持某种状态(如 remain unknown = 仍然未知)
- remain to be done = 有待被做(尚未发生)

---

### 【错误原因总结】

1. **固定搭配不熟**:compete for、remain to be seen 等高频表达需要重点记忆。
2. **非谓语动词的时态/体判断**:第 2、3、8 题全部是非谓语错误。要区分 to do / to be doing / to have done / to have been doing 的细微差别。
3. **反身代词**:govern oneself = 自治,主语和宾语是同一对象时用反身代词。
4. **从句引导词辨析**:as if(好像)vs in which;whose(所有格)vs who。看到空后是名词 → 考虑所有格 whose。
