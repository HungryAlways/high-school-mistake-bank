## 📝 错题 #1

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：语法填空
**知识点**：被动语态, 代词用法, 非谓语动词, 从句引导词, 介词短语

### 【题目原文】
MOSS, the first large-scale conversational language model in China, will be made an open-source software by the end of March, according to its research and development team. An open-source software (1) ______(release) under a license in which the copyright holder grants users the rights to use, study, change and distribute the software and (2) ______ source code.

(3) ______ (name) after the artificial intelligence-based computer (4) ______ controls the space station in the popular Chinese sci-fi film The Wandering Earth II, MOSS became the first ChatGPT-like software to be unveiled in China. It was made available for public testing on Feb 20th. Many people were quick (5) ______ (test) the software and share their experiences online. The traffic was so heavy that the platform had to be suspended for an upgrade (6) ______ the overload.

"The plan is to have MOSS interact with humans for a month so as to optimize it. (7) ______ everything goes well, it will be made open source by the end of March," Qiu Xipeng, director of the Natural Language Processing Committee of the Shanghai Computer Society, said on Sunday.

The domestically developed chatbot has opened up all the technical avenues for generative language models to understand human intentions and have dialogue capabilities, he noted (8) ______(add) that the project has received strong support from the Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory.

"Large-scale language models are almost monopolized by foreign countries." Qiu said. "(9) ______ we want is to share MOSS and its model codes and development experience with everyone, and hope that China (10) ______ be at the forefront of the world in terms of large-scale language models."

### 【你的答案】
1. will release
2. on
3. Named ✅
4. which ✅
5. testing
6. before
7. That
8. had added
9. So
10. will ✅

### 【正确答案】
1. is released
2. its
3. Named
4. which
5. to test
6. because of
7. If
8. adding
9. What
10. can

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **时态+语态错误**：(1) 客观事实用一般现在时被动语态 is released，不是将来时
2. **代词混淆**：(2) its source code = 它的源代码，its 指代 open-source software
3. **固定搭配不熟**：(5) quick to do sth，不是 quick doing
4. **介词短语误用**：(6) because of 表原因，不是 before 表时间
5. **从句引导词错误**：(7) If 引导条件状语从句，不是 That
6. **非谓语动词误用**：(8) adding 作伴随状语，不是过去完成时
7. **主语从句混淆**：(9) What 引导主语从句 = 我们想要的东西，不是 So

### 【解题思路】
1. **is released**：客观事实，一般现在时被动语态
2. **its**：代词，its source code = 它的源代码
3. **Named**：过去分词作状语，"以...命名"
4. **which**：定语从句，修饰 computer
5. **to test**：quick to do sth 固定搭配
6. **because of**：介词短语表原因
7. **If**：条件状语从句
8. **adding**：现在分词作伴随状语，补充说明
9. **What**：主语从句，What we want = 我们想要的东西
10. **can**：hope that China can be
