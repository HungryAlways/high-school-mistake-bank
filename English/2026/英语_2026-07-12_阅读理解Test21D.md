## 📝 错题 · Test 21 Part D（应用文阅读 / 招募广告）

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：应用文阅读（广告类：猜义 + 细节 + 推断 + 体裁）

### 【完整原文】
**D**

Do you want to get home from work knowing you have made a real difference in someone's life? If yes, don't care about sex or age! Come and join us, then <u>you'll make it</u>!

| | |
|---|---|
| **Position** | Volunteer Social Care Assistant（No Pay with Free Meals） |
| **Place** | Manchester |
| **Hours** | Part Time. We are now looking for volunteers to support people with learning disabilities to live active lives! Only 4 days left. Don't miss the chance of lending your warm hands to help others! |
| **Role** | You will provide people with learning disabilities with all aspects of their daily lives. You will help them to develop new skills. You will help them to protect their rights and their safety. But your primary concern is to let them know they are valued. |

### 【题目 + 选项】
1. What does the underlined part（you'll make it）mean?
   A. You'll make others' lives more meaningful with this job.
   B. You'll arrive home just in time from this job.
   C. You'll earn a good salary from this job.
   D. You'll succeed in getting this job.

2. The volunteers' major responsibility is to help people with learning disabilities ___.
   A. to get some financial support
   B. to properly protect themselves
   C. to learn some new living skills
   D. to realize their own importance

3. Which of the following can first be chosen as a volunteer?
   A. The one who can drive a car.
   B. The one who has done similar work before.
   C. The one who has patience to listen to others.
   D. The one who can use English to communicate.

4. The text serves as ___
   A. a reminder to social workers
   B. an advertisement for helpers
   C. a document on appealing for volunteers
   D. an introduction about a social care organization

### 【你的答案 vs 正确答案】

| 题 | 你的答案 | 标准答案 | 对错 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | A | A | ✅ |
| 2 | D | D | ✅ |
| 3 | B | B | ✅ |
| 4 | C | **B** | ❌ |

**得分：3/4**（只错第 4 题）

### 【逐题分析】

**第1题 ✅ → A**
- 猜义：`you'll make it` 在此语境（做志愿者、给他人生活带来改变）意为"你会做到 / 你会让它有意义"，呼应开头 made a real difference in someone's life → A。
- D "成功得到这份工作"是 make it 的另一常见义，但脱离本文"帮助他人"的语境，属于干扰。

**第2题 ✅ → D**
- 定位 Role 栏：`your primary concern is to let them know they are valued`（首要任务是让他们知道自己是被重视的）→ D to realize their own importance。

**第3题 ✅ → B**
- 定位 Skills 栏末句：`Previous care-related experience will be a great advantage for you`（有相关护理经验者优先）→ B 做过类似工作者最先被选。
- A 会开车、D 会英语都是"必备要求"而非"优先"；C 耐心倾听是基本要求。区分"required（必需）"和"advantage（加分/优先）"是关键。

**第4题 → B an advertisement for helpers（你选 C a document on appealing for volunteers）**
- 体裁/功能题。全文是一则"招募志愿者护理助理"的广告——有职位、地点、工时、职责、任职要求、申请方式，典型招募广告格式。
- B"招募帮手的广告"精准命中体裁(advertisement)+目的(找 helpers)。
- C 错在两点：① 体裁是"广告"而非"文件/document"；② appeal for（呼吁）偏情感号召，本文是实打实招人。
- 技巧：看文本长相——有职位名/任职要求/申请方式 = advertisement，不是 report/document/letter。

### 【涉及知识点】
1. **猜义题**（第1题）：短语义须扣具体语境（make it 在"帮助他人"语境=做到有意义的事）。
2. **细节定位**（第2、3题）：primary concern→首要职责；区分 required（必需）vs advantage（优先/加分）。
3. **体裁功能题**（第4题）：招募广告有职位+要求+申请方式 → advertisement，排除 document/appeal。

### 【举一反三】
1. 体裁判断：含 Position / Place / Hours / How to apply 的文本 → 招募广告(advertisement)。
2. required vs advantage：题问"谁优先被选"，找 advantage/preferred 附近的条件，而非 must/required 的硬性条件。
