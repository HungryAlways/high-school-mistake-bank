## 📝 错题 #1

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:Unit 3 Progress? / 核心词汇与语境填空
**知识点**:固定搭配、词性转换、语境辨析

### 【题目原文】
【词库】figures, threaten, capital, rated, continual, familiarize, populated, expertise, race, alarm, imaginary

1. Some marathon runners may suffer from kidney injury after finishing the **race**.
2. Lack of fear of nature can partly account for the ______ outbreak of natural disasters across the world.
3. Many parents of preschoolers struggle with their children's fears of ______ creatures.
4. The following guidelines and suggestions will ______ you with active reading techniques.
5. When it comes to the causes of stress, remember that your brain comes with a(n) ______ system for your protection.
6. Not only is the Hongkong Ocean Park the largest in Asia, but it is also ______ as one of the most spectacular oceanariums in the world.
7. The new strategy involves offering tech start-ups free rent, reduced tax rates, easier visa policies, and access to government-backed venture ______.
8. According to research conducted by Engma HR Co. LTD, 55.6 percent of HR professionals say they value a graduate's abilities over their specific area of ______.
9. This project was intended to save one of these dying regions - the remote and sparsely ______ regions - where the way of life had declined to a point of desperation.
10. Among the Committee's 51 members are top-university presidents, scholars, and business executives, as well as distinguished ______ from diplomacy, filmmaking, music and journalism.

### 【你的答案】
1. race | 2. ______ | 3. ______ | 4. ______ | 5. ______ | 6. populated | 7. rated | 8. ______ | 9. capital | 10. ______

### 【正确答案】
1. **race** | 2. **continual** | 3. **imaginary** | 4. **familiarize** | 5. **alarm** | 6. **rated** | 7. **capital** | 8. **expertise** | 9. **populated** | 10. **figures**

### 【错误原因分析】
**词库内混淆**:rated / capital / populated 三个词都在同一词库中,互换了位置,说明对它们的固定搭配不够熟悉:
- **rated** → be rated as(被评为)
- **capital** → venture capital(风险投资)
- **populated** → sparsely populated(人口稀少的)

### 【涉及知识点】
固定搭配、词性转换、语境辨析

### 【解题思路】
1. 先通读全题,标记已知搭配(如 race、continual、imaginary 等)
2. 对不确定的题,先看空格前后的**固定搭配信号词**:
   - "venture ___" → 固定术语 → **capital**
   - "be ___ as" → 被动 → **rated**
   - "sparsely ___" → 副词修饰 → **populated**
3. 排除法:词库中每个词只用一次,确定一个划掉一个

### 💡 举一反三
1. **be rated as** → The city is rated as one of the safest in the world.
2. **venture capital** → The startup raised $5 million in venture capital.
3. **sparsely populated** → Tibet is a sparsely populated region.

## 📝 错题 #2

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:Test 9 / 选词填空
**知识点**:词性辨析、固定搭配、上下文逻辑推理

### 【题目原文】
**词库**:A. understanding | B. encourages | C. results | D. flexible | E. returned | F. hunger | G. motivating | H. constantly | I. satisfied | J. supposed | K. combined

What is leadership? Its qualities are difficult to define, but they are not so difficult to identify. Leaders don't force other people to go along with them. They bring them along. Leaders get trust from others by giving it themselves, by building an environment that (1)______ creativity, and by operating with honesty and fairness.

Good leaders don't work alone. They recognize that an organization's strategies for success require the (2)______ talents and efforts of members. Leadership is the catalyst for transforming those talents into (3)______.

Successful leaders are emotionally and intellectually looking into the future-not stuck in the past. They have a (an) (4)______ to take responsibility and to innovate. They are not (5)______ with merely taking care of what has already been there. They want to move forward to create something new.

Leaders provide answers as well as direction, offer strength as well as devotion, and speak from experience as well as (6)______ of the problems they face and the people they work with.

Leaders are (7)______ rather than absolute. They believe in unity rather than yielding. And they strive to achieve agreements out of conflict.

Leadership is all about getting people (8)______ to give their best, helping them to grow to their fullest potential, and motivating them to work toward a common good. Leaders make the right things happen when they're (9)______ to.

A good leader, an effective leader, is one who has respect. Respect is something you have to have in order to receive. A leader who has respect for other people at all levels of an organization, for the work they do, and for their abilities, desires and needs, will find that respect is (10)______ and all concerned will be motivated to work together.

### 【你的答案】
1. G | 2. J | 3. ______ | 4. ______ | 5. ______ | 6. ______ | 7. ______ | 8. ______ | 9. K | 10. ______

### 【正确答案】
1. **B. encourages** | 2. **K. combined** | 3. **C. results** | 4. **F. hunger** | 5. **I. satisfied** | 6. **A. understanding** | 7. **D. flexible** | 8. **G. motivating** | 9. **J. supposed** | 10. **E. returned**

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **第 1 空**:that 引导定语从句修饰 environment,需要填**谓语动词**。encourages 是动词第三人称单数,作谓语正确;motivating 是现在分词,不能单独作谓语。
2. **第 2 空**:空格修饰 talents and efforts,需要形容词。combined talents and efforts = 共同的才能和努力。supposed 虽然也是形容词,但 "supposed talents" 语义不通。
3. **第 9 空**:be supposed to = **应该**,固定搭配。combined to 语义不通。

### 【涉及知识点】
词性辨析、固定搭配、定语从句、上下文逻辑推理

### 【解题思路】
1. 先判断空格词性(名词/形容词/动词等)
2. 再看搭配信号词(如 be ___ to → supposed to)
3. 结合上下文逻辑确定词义
4. 排除法:词库中每个词只用一次,确定一个划掉一个

### 💡 举一反三
1. **be supposed to** → Students are supposed to finish their homework on time.
2. **combined efforts** → With combined efforts, we can solve this problem.
3. **an environment that encourages...** → 定语从句谓语动词要注意主谓一致。

## 📝 错题 #3

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:Exercise 2 单句生词填空（B套）
**知识点**:固定搭配、词性辨析、并列结构、语境推理

### 【题目原文】
**词库**:diverse | trade | definite | compare | communicated | grateful | exchanged | translate | majoring | faithful | covering

1. For most westerners, nothing can **(1)______** with the taste of bread fresh from the oven.
2. Hello, Mr. Williams. Are you in a position to give us a(n) **(2)______** assurance that the goods will be delivered on time?
3. Jackson is a senior **(3)______** in biology and pre-medicine, hoping to obtain advanced degrees in public health and medicine.
4. At Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, scientists demonstrated a device last month that uses bacteria to **(4)______** pressure into electricity.
5. Even if some producers have delivered a large number of historically **(5)______** movies, expecting textbook accuracy from movies would be ridiculous.
6. China used to **(6)______** with central, south, and west Asia through the Silk Road in ancient times, which made the country a place that people all over the world longed for.
7. Not able to speak a foreign language, he could do nothing but express himself by means of **(7)______** body languages, which was really awkward.
8. On the advice of a friend who had already left London, they **(8)______** their crowded flats for a spacious apartment in Newquay overlooking Fistral Bay.
9. That teacher often makes across-time connections, reminding students of how something they are doing or **(9)______** today relates to previous activities and content covered.
10. The Tennessee and Campbell County Health Departments are monitoring this patient and have declared that this patient has not **(10)______** the virus to any other person.

### 【你的答案】
1. ______ | 2. ______ | 3. ______ | 4. ______ | 5. ______ | 6. covering | 7. ______ | 8. ______ | 9. grateful | 10. ______

### 【正确答案】
1. **compare** | 2. **definite** | 3. **majoring** | 4. **translate** | 5. **faithful** | 6. **trade** | 7. **communicated** | 8. **exchanged** | 9. **covering** | 10. **grateful**

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **第 6 空**:used to **trade** with = 过去常常与...贸易。used to do 表过去习惯,需要填动词原形。covering 虽然也是动词原形,但 "used to covering with" 语义不通,且 trade with 是固定搭配。
2. **第 9 空**:something they are doing or **covering** → doing 和 covering 并列,cover 在这里表示"学习/涵盖(内容)"。grateful 是形容词,不能与 doing 并列。

### 【涉及知识点】
固定搭配、词性辨析、并列结构、语境推理

### 【解题思路】
1. 先判断空格词性(名词/形容词/动词等)
2. 再看搭配信号词(如 used to do → 填动词原形)
3. 注意并列结构(doing or covering → 词性一致)
4. 排除法:词库中每个词只用一次,确定一个划掉一个

### 💡 举一反三
1. **used to do** → I used to play football when I was young.(过去常常做)
2. **trade with** → China used to trade with many countries along the Silk Road.
3. **doing or covering** → 并列结构要求词性一致,either doing or doing / either noun or noun
