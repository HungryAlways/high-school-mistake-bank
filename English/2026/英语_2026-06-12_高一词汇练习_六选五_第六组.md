# 高一词汇练习·六选五（第六组）

## 原题

第六组

A. desperate    B. undeniable    C. continent    D. corporation    AB. frustration    AC. decent

For years, our small tech team had been working in a state of 76. ______ need for a breakthrough. We knew our idea—a low-cost water purification device—could change lives in remote communities. However, progress was slow, and securing support felt almost impossible.

Then, we were invited to present our prototype to a major international 77. ______. It was our biggest chance yet. The first meeting, however, was a source of immense 78. ______. The executives seemed uninterested, focusing only on immediate profits rather than long-term impact.

We returned to our lab, discouraged but determined to refine our pitch. After months of adjustments, we had a 79. ______ model that was both efficient and easier to produce. A second meeting was arranged. This time, we led with data from field tests and powerful stories from the communities we aimed to serve.

The response was entirely different. The 80. ______ value of our project, both ethically and commercially, suddenly became clear to them. By the end of the quarter, we had secured the funding and partnership we needed. The journey taught us that perseverance could turn even the faintest idea into a force for good.

## 错题解析

### 第 77 题

#### 【错误原因分析】
本题错在混淆 **continent** 和 **corporation**。continent 表示“大陆、洲”，不能作为 present our prototype to 的对象；句中后文出现 **executives**，说明对方是一家公司或企业。

#### 【涉及知识点】
- corporation：公司，企业
- continent：大陆，洲
- international corporation：跨国公司/国际公司
- 语境词义辨析

#### 【解题思路】
句意：随后，我们被邀请向一家大型国际公司展示原型。根据 **present our prototype to** 和后文 **The executives** 可知，应选 **D. corporation**。

### 第 78 题

#### 【错误原因分析】
本题错在把 **corporation** 放入 **a source of immense...** 结构中。corporation 是具体名词“公司”，而第一次会议中高管不感兴趣，给团队带来的是“挫败感”，应使用 **frustration**。

#### 【涉及知识点】
- frustration：挫败感，沮丧
- corporation：公司
- a source of immense frustration：巨大挫败感的来源
- 抽象名词搭配

#### 【解题思路】
句意：然而，第一次会议是巨大挫败感的来源。根据后文 **The executives seemed uninterested** 可知会议结果令人沮丧，所以选 **AB. frustration**。

### 第 79 题

#### 【错误原因分析】
本题错在把 **undeniable** 用来修饰 model。undeniable 表示“不可否认的”，常修饰 fact、truth、value 等；这里描述经过数月调整后得到的模型“既高效又更容易生产”，应为 **decent**，表示“像样的、不错的”。

#### 【涉及知识点】
- decent：像样的，不错的，合格的
- undeniable：不可否认的
- a decent model：一个像样/不错的模型
- 形容词语境辨析

#### 【解题思路】
句意：经过几个月的调整，我们有了一个像样的模型，它既高效又更容易生产。根据 **both efficient and easier to produce** 可知，应选 **AC. decent**。

### 第 80 题

#### 【错误原因分析】
本题错在把 **decent** 用于修饰 value。decent 表示“像样的、不错的、体面的”，常修饰 job、person、model、living conditions 等；而项目价值变得“清楚且不可否认”，应使用 **undeniable value**。

#### 【涉及知识点】
- undeniable：不可否认的
- decent：像样的，不错的
- undeniable value：不可否认的价值
- 形容词搭配

#### 【解题思路】
句意：我们项目在伦理和商业上的不可否认的价值突然变得清楚了。横线后是 **value**，结合 **became clear to them** 可知，应选 **B. undeniable**。
