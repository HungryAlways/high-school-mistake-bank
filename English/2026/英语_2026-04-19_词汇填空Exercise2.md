## 📝 错题 #3

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:Exercise 2 单句生词填空（B套）
**知识点**:固定搭配、词性辨析、并列结构、语境推理

### 【题目原文】
**词库**:diverse | trade | definite | compare | communicated | grateful | exchanged | translate | majoring | faithful | covering

1. For most westerners, nothing can **(1)______** with the taste of bread fresh from the oven.
2. Hello, Mr. Williams. Are you in a position to give us a(n) **(2)______** assurance that the goods will be delivered on time?
3. Jackson is a senior **(3)______** in biology and pre-medicine, hoping to obtain advanced degrees in public health and medicine.
4. At Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, scientists demonstrated a device last month that uses bacteria to **(4)______** pressure into electricity.
5. Even if some producers have delivered a large number of historically **(5)______** movies, expecting textbook accuracy from movies would be ridiculous.
6. China used to **(6)______** with central, south, and west Asia through the Silk Road in ancient times, which made the country a place that people all over the world longed for.
7. Not able to speak a foreign language, he could do nothing but express himself by means of **(7)______** body languages, which was really awkward.
8. On the advice of a friend who had already left London, they **(8)______** their crowded flats for a spacious apartment in Newquay overlooking Fistral Bay.
9. That teacher often makes across-time connections, reminding students of how something they are doing or **(9)______** today relates to previous activities and content covered.
10. The Tennessee and Campbell County Health Departments are monitoring this patient and have declared that this patient has not **(10)______** the virus to any other person.

### 【你的答案】
1. ______ | 2. ______ | 3. ______ | 4. ______ | 5. ______ | 6. covering | 7. ______ | 8. ______ | 9. grateful | 10. ______

### 【正确答案】
1. **compare** | 2. **definite** | 3. **majoring** | 4. **translate** | 5. **faithful** | 6. **trade** | 7. **communicated** | 8. **exchanged** | 9. **covering** | 10. **grateful**

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **第 6 空**:used to **trade** with = 过去常常与...贸易。used to do 表过去习惯,需要填动词原形。covering 虽然也是动词原形,但 "used to covering with" 语义不通,且 trade with 是固定搭配。
2. **第 9 空**:something they are doing or **covering** → doing 和 covering 并列,cover 在这里表示"学习/涵盖(内容)"。grateful 是形容词,不能与 doing 并列。

### 【涉及知识点】
固定搭配、词性辨析、并列结构、语境推理

### 【解题思路】
1. 先判断空格词性(名词/形容词/动词等)
2. 再看搭配信号词(如 used to do → 填动词原形)
3. 注意并列结构(doing or covering → 词性一致)
4. 排除法:词库中每个词只用一次,确定一个划掉一个

### 💡 举一反三
1. **used to do** → I used to play football when I was young.(过去常常做)
2. **trade with** → China used to trade with many countries along the Silk Road.
3. **doing or covering** → 并列结构要求词性一致,either doing or doing / either noun or noun
