## 📝 错题 #2

**学科**:英语 | **章节**:综合英语 · 篇章选词填空
**知识点**:近义词辨析、固定搭配、上下文逻辑呼应、词性预判

### 【题目原文】

**Exercise 7 篇章选词填空**

A. effects  B. field  C. recognized  D. drawbacks  E. identity
F. paid  G. interact  H. motivation  I. flourish  J. function  K. dissatisfaction

**The Negative Effects and Aspects of Homeschooling**

More parents are choosing to homeschool their children because they see many benefits of homeschooling. Differences in ideology and (1) ___ with the current school education are only a few reasons why some parents choose homeschooling over public or private schools. While there are many positive aspects of homeschooling, there are also (2) ___ of homeschooling. In this article, we will explore more.

**Time**
When parents take the responsibility for educating their children at home, they may need to set aside time to make it work. The task of homeschooling a child is certainly not easy, especially for working parents, single parents or stay-at-home parents. They have to spend time organizing and preparing lessons, teaching, giving tests, and planning (3) ___ trips. Homeschooling is a full-time commitment and to make sure that the child receives a quality education, parents need to invest time and effort needed.

**Costs**
In comparison to public schools, where education is free, homeschooling can be costly. Purchasing the newest curriculum and teaching tools can be very expensive. Parents may choose to use a(n) (4) ___ homeschooling program, and such programs may bring added benefits, but may increase the costs of the child's education. There are also other costs to keep in mind, like project materials, stationery, books, and computer software, etc. Parents who choose to home school their children will undoubtedly spend more money than parents who send their children to public schools.

**Socialization**
Home schooled children may not have as many opportunities to (5) ___ with other children as children who attend regular schools do. Forming bonds, socializing with children of their own age and developing a sense of (6) ___ is important for the child's developmental health and development of social skills. If home-schooled, they may be deprived of the chance to form friendships and may suffer socially. Of course, they can make friends with other home-schooled children, but even if special efforts can be made to arrange online meetings, they can never (7) ___ as real social occasions where kid's social skills can get truly and fully cultivated. In fact, the lack of socialization may have negative side (8) ___ on them in later stages of life.

**Atmosphere**
Some children need to be challenged to excel in their studies. In this sense, they (9) ___ when they are involved in some competition. However, children who are homeschooled may not have this (10) ___ because most of them are educated separately. This is especially tricky for families with only one child at home.

### 【选项】
- A. effects
- B. field
- C. recognized
- D. drawbacks
- E. identity
- F. paid
- G. interact
- H. motivation
- I. flourish
- J. function
- K. dissatisfaction

### 【你的答案】
> 1. D · 2. K · 3. B · 4. F · 5. G · 6. H · 7. I · 8. A · 9. C · 10. E

### 【正确答案】
> 1. **K** · 2. **D** · 3. **B** · 4. **F** · 5. **G** · 6. **E** · 7. **C** · 8. **A** · 9. **I** · 10. **H**

### 【答题统计】
**6/10 正确** -- 对了 6 道,有 4 道近义词/搭配混淆

### 【错题逐题分析】

#### ❌ 第 1 题:D → K
> "Differences in ideology and **(1)** ___ with the current school education"

**为什么选 K(dissatisfaction):**
- "ideology and dissatisfaction" 并列,都接 "with the current school education"--即"意识形态差异"和"对当前教育的不满"
- 家长送孩子在家上学的原因:**不满** + **理念不同**
- drawbacks 不能和 ideology 并列,drawbacks 指事物本身的负面因素,不是人对某事物的态度

💡 **技巧**:并列结构看搭配--两个词要能同时接 "with"。dissatisfaction with = 对...的不满 ✓

---

#### ❌ 第 2 题:K → D
> "While there are many positive aspects..., there are also **(2)** ___ of homeschooling."

**为什么选 D(drawbacks):**
- **While** 引导转折--前面说 positive aspects(优点),后面自然接 negative aspects = **drawbacks**(缺点)
- drawback 与 positive aspect 形成完美对比

💡 **技巧**:While/although 这类转折词是信号词,前后意思相反。positive → negative 对应

---

#### ❌ 第 6 题:H → E
> "developing a sense of **(6)** ___ is important..."

**为什么选 E(identity):**
- **a sense of identity = 身份认同感**,是固定搭配,也是心理学高频词组
- Socialization 这一段讲的是社交能力发展,身份认同是重要组成部分

💡 **技巧**:看到 "a sense of ___",常见搭配:sense of identity(身份认同)、sense of belonging(归属感)、sense of humor(幽默感)。motivation 不跟 sense of 搭配。

---

#### ❌ 第 7 题:I → C
> "they can never **(7)** ___ as real social occasions..."

**为什么选 C(recognized):**
- "never **recognized as**" = "不会被认可为/被视为"
- 句意:线上聚会永远**不被认可为**真正的社交场合

💡 **技巧**:看后面的 **as**!"___ as" 结构中,recognized as(被认定为)最符合语境。flourish 是不及物动词,后面不接 as。

---

#### ❌ 第 9 题:C → I
> "they **(9)** ___ when they are involved in some competition."

**为什么选 I(flourish):**
- flourish = **茁壮成长、表现出色**
- 句意:有些孩子在竞争中**能取得更好的表现**
- 前文 "excel"(表现出色)与 flourish 是**同义呼应**

💡 **技巧**:看上下文呼应--challenged to **excel** → flourish 是同义链。recognized 是被动含义,不符合语境。

---

#### ❌ 第 10 题:E → H
> "children who are homeschooled may not have this **(10)** ___..."

**为什么选 H(motivation):**
- 前文提到竞争能激发动力 → homeschooled 孩子缺少这种 **motivation(动力)**
- "this motivation" 指代前文竞争带来的动力

💡 **技巧**:看指代--"this ___" 说明前面提到了类似概念。竞争 → 动力(motivation),逻辑通顺。

---

### 【错误原因总结】

1. **近义词辨析薄弱**:drawbacks(缺点/不利因素)vs dissatisfaction(不满情绪)--前者指事物本身的不足,后者指人的感受
2. **固定搭配积累不足**:a sense of identity、recognized as、side effects 等需要系统整理
3. **上下文呼应意识不够**:flourish 和 excel 是同义呼应,motivation 和 competition 是因果呼应--做题要**上下扫视**,不能只看空的那一句话
