# Unit 1 No Limits Exercise 8 完形填空 Why Don't Politicians Grow Facial Hair Anymore?

Various studies have shown that nowadays beards change the __3__ of men for the better. Men without beards are more likely to appear trustworthy and be thought to have greater knowledge while endorsing something. However, this was not the case a century ago.

In America, the last beard elected to office was President Benjamin Harrison in 1893, and the last president to wear any kind of facial hair was William Howard Taft, who had a(n) __4__ during his term from March 1909 to March 1913. This was the end of an era started by __5__ Abraham Lincoln. Lincoln was probably the only guy in the history of beards who decided to __6__ a no-mustache beard because of a letter from an 11-year-old girl who asked him to have some facial hair to cover up his thin face. They had found a continuous declining trend towards wearing hair around the mouth. Currently, facial hair is rare among modern politicians except those in the __9__ where Islamic traditions are held. It's estimated that fewer than five percent of the members of Congress have either beards or mustaches. Although there is very limited research into the __10__ between politician's facial hair and voting, there's been a couple of good guesses. Many political analysts have learned that many voters __11__ their choices on something trivial or superficial traits they see, and a man without a beard or moustache can give voters, especially female ones, a sense of security — looking less __12__ — and __13__ to lead. Facial hair was once __14__ to make a man look wise and powerful, but the age of __15__ has changed all that.

## 【逐题错误分析】

### 第 1 题
- **B representatives → C candidates**：本句 "our bearded ___ were out of luck in the election cycle"（我们留胡子的___在选举中不走运）。选举语境指"候选人"，用 **candidates**。representatives（代表）范围不准；characters（人物/性格）、presidents（总统，太具体）均不合。

### 第 2 题
- **C half-beard → D clean-shaven**：与前文"not a beard in sight"（看不到胡子）呼应，政客们总是保持"干净剃须"的外貌，用 **clean-shaven**（脸刮得干净的）。half-beard（半胡子）与"看不到胡子"矛盾。

### 第 3 题
- **B sight → A perception**：胡子"改变人们对男性的___"，指改变**看法/观感**，用 **perception**（认知、看法）。后文 appear trustworthy / be thought to have knowledge 都是"别人怎么看他"，正是 perception。sight（视力/景象）、values（价值观）、uniqueness（独特性）均不贴。

### 第 4 题
- **D make-up → B mustache**：Taft 是"最后一位留面部毛发的总统"，had a(n) ___ 应是**胡子** mustache。make-up（化妆）、jewel（珠宝）、eyelid（眼皮）都非面部毛发。

### 第 5 题（✓）
- **C none other than**（不是别人正是）：This was the end of an era started by **none other than** Abraham Lincoln（由林肯本人开创的时代）。强调"正是林肯"，用对。

### 第 6 题（✓）
- **D grow out**（把……留长）：decided to **grow out** a no-mustache beard（决定蓄起没有小胡子的络腮胡）。grow out 表"（让胡须/头发）长出、留长"，用对。

### 第 7 题（✓）
- **A after all**（毕竟）：破折号后对前句补充说明——"毕竟高贵的人常被认为长得好看"，用 **after all** 表因果性解释，用对。

### 第 8 题
- **A early → C late**：sociologists "tracked facial hair trends from the ___ 1800s to the 1970s"。与后面 1970s 构成一个较近的时间跨度，且下文说面部毛发呈"持续下降趋势"，起点应是 **late** 1800s（19世纪末）。early 1800s（19世纪初）跨度过大且不合史实（前文林肯、Taft 都在19世纪末20世纪初）。

### 第 9 题
- **B East Asia → C Middle East**：面部毛发在现代政客中少见，"except those in the ___ where Islamic traditions are held"（除了保有伊斯兰传统的___地区）。伊斯兰传统对应 **Middle East**（中东），不是 East Asia（东亚）。

### 第 10 题
- **B range → D relativity**：research into the ___ between facial hair and voting（研究胡子与投票之间的___）。指两者的**关联/相关性**。四选项里 **relativity**（相关性/相对关系）最贴（此处取"关联"义）。range（范围）、dynamics（动态）、differences（差异）都不能准确表达"两者之间的关系"。

### 第 11 题
- **A fix → D base**：many voters ___ their choices **on** something trivial（选民把选择___在琐碎的特征上）。固定搭配 **base sth on sth**（把……建立在……之上）。fix on 意为"注视/确定"，搭配和语义都不对；blame on、impose on 语义均不合。

### 第 12 题（✓）
- **B aggressive**（好斗的/咄咄逼人的）：无胡子的男人给选民（尤其女性）安全感——"looking less **aggressive**"（显得不那么咄咄逼人）。用对。

### 第 13 题
- **A forced → B entitled**：与前面 less aggressive 并列，"and (more) ___ to lead"（更有资格领导）。**entitled to do**（有资格/有权做）搭配正确，语义顺。forced to lead（被迫领导）与"给人安全感"的褒义语境矛盾。

### 第 14 题
- **A applied → C intended**：Facial hair was once ___ to make a man look wise（面部毛发曾经被___用来让男人显得睿智）。**be intended to do**（旨在/被用来做）表目的。applied to（应用于）搭配和语义都不如 intended 贴切。

### 第 15 题
- **C internet → D televisions**："the age of ___ has changed all that"（___时代改变了这一切）。文章讲的是选民能

## 【知识点】

1. **选举/政治词汇**：candidate（候选人）、clean-shaven（剃净胡须的）、mustache（小胡子）、beard（络腮胡）。
2. **动词固定搭配**：
   - **base sth on sth**（把……建立在……之上，第11题）
   - **be intended to do**（旨在做，第14题）
   - **be entitled to do**（有资格做，第13题）
   - **grow out**（蓄长，第6题）
3. **语义辨析类**：perception（看法）vs sight（视力）；relativity/关联 vs range/dynamics；aggressive（咄咄逼人）。
4. **逻辑衔接**：
   - **none other than**（正是，强调）
   - **after all**（毕竟，补充解释因果）
5. **常识+时间推理**：late 1800s（结合林肯、Taft 时代）；Middle East + Islamic traditions（宗教文化背景）；televisions（视觉媒体让"看脸"成为可能）。
6. **完形做题法**：抓住上下文呼应词（如 not a beard in sight → clean-shaven；less aggressive → entitled to lead），并结合固定搭配和常识排除。
