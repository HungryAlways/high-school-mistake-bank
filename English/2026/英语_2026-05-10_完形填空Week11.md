## 📝 错题 #3

**学科**：英语 | **章节**：完形填空

### 【题目原文】
**Week 11 — Cloze**

Directions: For each blank in the following passage there are four words or phrases marked A, B, C and D. Fill in each blank with the word or phrase that best fits the context.

---

"Every technology has improved over the years except batteries! Why can't someone invent a better battery?" These are the words we often hear from technology fans. In fact, though, the average technology fan is **(21) ______** two huge points about batteries.

First point: The batteries you probably think about most are the ones in your phone or laptop. But you could argue—and many scientists do—that batteries are the **(22) ______** to handling much, much bigger problems, like energy, transportation and climate change.

**(23) ______**, today electric cars represent only about 1 percent of U.S. new car sales. One reason is that they cost more than gas-powered cars. Another is range **(24) ______**—consumers' fear that they'll run out of charge far from home. The **(25) ______** higher-capacity batteries now under development aim to solve both of those problems.

Then there's the grid (输电网). Electric utilities spend their days coping with swings in energy **(26) ______**. There's almost no demand at night, when everyone's asleep, and then tremendous increase at 5 p.m., when people get home from work. Utilities actually **(27) ______** expensive, inefficient, occasionally used backup power plants just to handle demand increase, as occurs during heat waves.

Batteries connected to the grid could **(28) ______** those strange swings. Maybe even more important, grid batteries could capture solar power while the sun's shining—and wind power when it's blowing—for use when we really need it. Thus far we haven't been able to make the sun and wind respect our lifestyle **(29) ______**.

The other important point: Batteries have been getting better over the decades. The reason why we don't **(30) ______** it is that our devices have been getting faster, more powerful and more power-hungry at the same time. If you could put a **(31) ______** iPhone battery into a 1995 phone, it'd probably go a year on a single charge.

Other great things are **(32) ______**. Materials scientist Mike Zimmerman has succeeded in replacing the liquid electrolyte (液体电解质), which can burn easily, with a single piece of special plastic film. That is, batteries are **(33) ______** exploding. And because it's unblowuppable, Zimmerman can use lithium metal, which has a much higher energy density but is considered too **(34) ______** to use with today's liquid-electrolyte batteries.

So if you do want to complain about your batteries, get it out now. It won't be long before they have a much better **(35) ______**. Let's wait and see!

### 【你的答案】
21. D | 22. C | 23. A | 24. A | 25. C | 26. A | 27. C | 28. C | 29. B | 30. A | 31. D | 32. C | 33. D | 34. B | 35. C

### 【正确答案】
21. C.missing | 22. A.keys | 23. D.For example | 24. A.anxiety | 25. B.cheaper | 26. B.demand | 27. A.maintain | 28. D.even out | 29. C.schedules | 30. A.notice | 31. D.modern | 32. C.on the way | 33. B.incapable of | 34. B.risky | 35. B.opportunity

### 【错误原因分析】
1. **(21) weighing → missing**：missing = 忽略、没注意到。人们抱怨电池没进步，但实际上**忽略了**两个重要事实。weighing = 权衡，语义不符。
2. **(22) resistances → keys**：**keys to** = …的关键。电池是解决能源、交通等大问题的**关键**。resistances = 阻力，语义完全相反。
3. **(23) In conclusion → For example**：电动车是前文"能源、交通、气候变化"这些大问题的**具体例子**，用 For example。In conclusion（总之）用于总结，这里不是总结。
4. **(25) safer → cheaper**：前文提到两个问题：电动车**成本更高**（cost more）和**里程焦虑**。更高容量 + 规模效应 → 电池**更便宜**，从而解决价格问题。
5. **(26) price → demand**：后文解释：晚上几乎没需求，下午 5 点大幅增加——这是能源**需求**的波动。energy demand = 能源需求，固定搭配。
6. **(27) abandon → maintain**：**maintain** = 维持、运营。公用事业公司需要**维持**这些备用电厂以应对需求高峰。
7. **(28) pick out → even out**：**even out** = 使平稳、消除波动。电网电池可以**平抑**能源需求的波动。
8. **(29) directions → schedules**：**schedules** = 时间表。太阳不会因为我们下班才出来，风也不会因为我们需要才吹。
9. **(33) sensitive to → incapable of**：用特殊塑料薄膜代替易燃液体电解质 → 电池**无法**爆炸了。**incapable of** = 不能……的。
10. **(35) investment → opportunity**：电池很快会有更好的**前景/机会**。结合上下文，电池技术即将迎来突破。

### 【涉及知识点】
- 词义辨析：missing / weighing / maintain / abandon
- 固定搭配：keys to / range anxiety / energy demand / even out / incapable of / on the way
- 逻辑连接词：For example（举例）vs In conclusion（总结）
- 上下文推理：前文 cost more → 后文 cheaper 解决价格问题
- 短语辨析：even out（使平稳）/ find out（找出）/ pick out（挑选）/ hand out（分发）

### 【解题思路】
1. **通读全文**：先了解文章主题（电池技术被误解），把握整体逻辑。
2. **判断逻辑关系**：举例（For example）还是总结（In conclusion）？
3. **找固定搭配**：range anxiety / energy demand / even out / incapable of。
4. **上下文呼应**：前文提到 cost more → 后文 cheaper 解决价格问题。
5. **排除干扰项**：有些选项单个看似乎合理，但需要结合全文逻辑判断。
6. **注意词义细微差别**：maintain（维持运营）vs abandon（放弃）vs identify（识别）。
